LONG CHIREY OR CHATNI
long churhay ki chatni
long chirhay ki chatni
long chirhay
long churhay

INGREDIENTS FOR CHATNI :
whole dried mangoes / sabut khatai /sabut amchor 50 gm
tamarind / imli pulp 50 gm
salt 1 tsp
red chili flakes 2 tsp
cumin seeds 1 tsp

INGREDIENTS FOR YELLOW FRITTERS :
gram flour 2 cups / 240 gm
rice flour 2 tbsp
baking soda 2 pinch
salt 1 tsp
red chili pwd 1/2 tsp

INGREDIENTS FOR RED FRITTERS :
gram flour 1 cup
rice flour 1 tbsp
salt 1/2 tsp
red chili pwd 1/4 tsp
baking soda 1 pinch
orange, red food color as required

INGREDIENTS FOR SEEKH KABAB :
gram flour 1 cup
rice flour 1 tbsp
salt 1/4 tsp
turmeric 1/4 tsp
chili flakes 1/2 tbsp or to taste
crushed coriander 1/2 tsp
cumin seeds 1/2 tsp
fresh chopped coriander 1 tsp
grated , squeezed onion 1 tbsp heaped
oil 1 tbsp
