"Pick" is a very common verb. It means to select one or more from many.

We can turn "pick" into a phrasal verb and use "pick out" to have the same meaning.

"Pick up" as a phrasal verb has a different meaning. Itâ€™s used to talk about lifting something with your hands and to talk about going to a location to get someone or something.
