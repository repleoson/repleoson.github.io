Call of Duty Warzone is gearing up for the next era of Battle Royale and the subsidiary modes. With Vanguard’s integration coming up soon, Warzone looks to set sail and head out to the Pacific in the Warzone Pacific experience on the brand new map Caldera. However, before the Vanguard integration happens with Warzone and we say goodbye to Verdansk forever, we have a series of three events happening across the next few weeks to properly call it quits on the map that we’ve played for the last year and a half. The first of which is Operation: Flashback, a new mode and event coming to Warzone this week that looks to take a look back on the last year and a half of action, excitement and mystery. Operation: Flashback in Warzone is going to allow players to experience a little bit of everything from the time we’ve had in Verdansk, but all in one singular game, with each new circle rumored to bring us a new event that makes for some crazy action and some memorable matches. For Operation: Flashback, there really isn’t a ton of information out there right now so today I wanted to break down everything you need to know about Operation: Flashback in Warzone coming later this week. What can you expect? When will it go live? Is there anything specific granted for playing the mode? What happens next? We’ll break down all of that an more in this video.
