The first of which is Operation: Flashback, a new mode and event coming to Warzone this week that looks to take a look back on the last year and a half of action, excitement and mystery. Operation: Flashback in Warzone is going to allow players to experience a little bit of everything from the time we’ve had in Verdansk, but all in one singular game, with each new circle rumored to bring us a new event that makes for some crazy action and some memorable matches. For Operation: Flashback, there really isn’t a ton of information out there right now so today I wanted to break down everything you need to know about Operation: Flashback in Warzone coming later this week.
