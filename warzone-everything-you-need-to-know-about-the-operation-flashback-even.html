The first of which is Operation: Flashback, a new mode and event coming to Warzone this week that looks to take a look back on the last year and a half of action, excitement and mystery. For Operation: Flashback, there really isn’t a ton of information out there right now so today I wanted to break down everything you need to know about Operation: Flashback in Warzone coming later this week.
