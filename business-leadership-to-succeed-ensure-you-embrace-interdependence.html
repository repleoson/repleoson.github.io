For a growing company to succeed, its leaders have to learn to embrace multi-faceted inter-relations with individuals who possess sought-after skills.

Zilwa has not only read up extensively on this phenomenon, but also has a wealth of first-hand experience on the subject, thanks in no small part to the fact that she heads up a 400-person organisation.
