The Mill House is comprised of 3 sections - palm oil, palm kernel oil and briquettes processing.

Under a contract awarded by Solidaridad, FINIC built the mill and all the equipment installed in all of its 3 sections.

The palm oil section has capacity to process 1 ton of Fresh Fruits Bunches per hour. That will be equivalent to 1,500 litres of oil per day of 8 hours.

This video is not a working video but rather, one that shows the equipment layout. The working video will be uploaded later.
