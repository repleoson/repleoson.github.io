Let’s see your happy face! How about a sleepy face? Can you make an excited face? Let’s see a silly face! This song is great for teaching emotions. For children who have challenges recognizing and reading facial expressions, it’s a great chance to become familiar with a variety of physical emotional cues in a fun way.
