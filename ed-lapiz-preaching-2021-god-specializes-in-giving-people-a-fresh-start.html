"PRIVATE AUDIENCE"
Matthew 6:6
When you pray, go into a room alone and close the door. Pray to your Father in private. He knows what is done in private, and he will reward you.
Make a lot of effort to pray privately. Gusto mong manalangin? Pumunta ka sa iyong silid, isara mo ang pinto at nang sa ganun, ang pakikipagniig mo sa Dios ay pribado, tahimik at lihim.
