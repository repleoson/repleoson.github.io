The video shows a bespoke robotic packaging solution delivered by PWR to Yummy Bakery in Belgium for picking and placing pancakes in a thermoformer.

The PWR solution, comprising of 3 robots, is designed for high speed robotic picking and placing of round and other shapes of pancakes. The solution can pick over 12.000 pancakes per hour. The solution is supported by two 2D vision systems to accurately determine the position and orientation of each pancake and ensure optimal performance and quality control.

PWR are specialists in developing automated robotic packaging solutions for the food industry, which improve the performance of production lines. We work together with our customers to develop bespoke solutions that are specific to your needs. We guarantee our modular systems will give you the highest possible flexibility, efficiency, reliability and clear cost savings.
