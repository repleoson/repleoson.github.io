After summer 2021, we emerged from the pandemic with a revitalised energy, and whilst exciting changes have been made inside the club, we have also made changes to our programming ethos and are excited to return to extended events again…

On Saturday 20th November, we’ll launch ‘Continuum’ – a new series of 24+ hour parties for 2021-22, harking back to the foundations the venue was originally founded upon. The ‘Continuum’ series of events promises a non-stop sonic journey, exploring a range of genres with names from across the electronic music spectrum.
