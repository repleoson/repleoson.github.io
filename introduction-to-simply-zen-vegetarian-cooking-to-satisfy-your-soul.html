Kakuho Aoe, a Japanese Buddhist monk and also temple cook, presents you his first recipe book, Simply Zen: Vegetarian Cooking to Satisfy Your Soul.

As a result, the food prepared in Zen monasteries is healthy, low-calorie, accentuates the most subtle of flavors, and can even be eaten to help detox the body.
