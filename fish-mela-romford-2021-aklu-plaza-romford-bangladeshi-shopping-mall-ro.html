Aklu Plaza
Aklu Plaza is a three storey shopping mall situated at the heart of Romford town centre, boasting over 200000 sqft of retail space located approximately 0.4 miles (0.6 km) north of Romford National Rail station on the Elizabeth Line (Crossrail). The Liberty Shopping Centre, Brewery Shopping Centre and Mercury Shopping Centre are all within close proximity. Excellent transport links, providing easy access to Aklu Plaza.

Location

Romford is a rapidly evolving town on the borders of East London and Essex which has seen significant redevelopment in recent years following the success of the Olympic Park
redevelopment in Stratford.
The Liberty Shopping Centre, Brewery Shopping Centre and Mercury Shopping Centre are all within close proximity. Established stores such as B&M, Home Burgain, AlDI, Iceland, H&M, JD Sports, M&S, Primark, Boots are all within walking distance. These provide a comprehensive retail offering for Romford Town Centre.
Excellent transport links, providing easy access west towards Central London and east towards Brentwood and Essex.
