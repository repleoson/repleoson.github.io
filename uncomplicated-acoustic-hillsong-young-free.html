All Of My Best Friends (Acoustic) by Hillsong Young & Free

"We made ‘All Of My Best Friends (Acoustic)’ during a season where we found ourselves singing these songs, more often than not, on a piano or an acoustic guitar. Although they were designed to be sung in live rooms with many people jumping and dancing, we found that there was an intimacy that came when we sung these songs this way. This album represents this season, our closeness to God and our need of Him." – Aodhan King
