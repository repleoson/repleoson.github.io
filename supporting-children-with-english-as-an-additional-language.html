This essential session will examine the stages of language development that children with English as an additional language (EAL) may go through. Join the Alliance’s Specialist Early Years Teachers, Lynsey Weston and Sian Ansell to consider the EAL parent and child experience of coming to an early years setting. The session will also explore practical ways in which children and parents/carers who have EAL can be supported.
