Persistence is an interoperable protocol built to facilitate the creation of next-gen financial products powered by blockchain. The Persistence tech stack provides the infrastructure to effectively bridge the realms of DeFi and traditional financial in a variety of areas, whilst also enabling the creation of innovative crypto-facing solutions to expand the DeFi space.

Using the features of DeFi and utilizing NFTs to tokenize real-world assets can solve the current problems of traditional finance, thus opening the floodgates to allow a torrent of new capital into the crypto ecosystem.

The first DApp in the Persistence ecosystem is Comdex, an institutional-facing commodities trading and trade financing platform which has handled over $41M in transaction volume since launch. Comdex revolutionizes the $17 trillion commodity trading industry by tying banking structures with blockchain via the tokenization of trade invoices.

Comdex is just one use case of Persistence. Our SDK toolkit can be used for a wide range of use cases e.g. general purpose NFT marketplaces, solar credit trading, and much more. We aim to create a whole suite of financial products, many of which are currently underway.
