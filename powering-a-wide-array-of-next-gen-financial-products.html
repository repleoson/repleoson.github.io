Persistence is an interoperable protocol built to facilitate the creation of next-gen financial products powered by blockchain. Comdex is just one use case of Persistence. Our SDK toolkit can be used for a wide range of use cases e.g. general purpose NFT marketplaces, solar credit trading, and much more. We aim to create a whole suite of financial products, many of which are currently underway.
