MBK CENTER has various sales corners.
There is a cheap and delicious food court, a corner that sells cute children’s clothes, and a corner that sells casual clothes such as T-shirts.

There is also a food court on this floor, so it’s a good place to have a meal or a break after shopping at the souvenir corner.
It’s fun just looking around the souvenir corner.
