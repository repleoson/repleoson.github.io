Dr. Berg talks about how to reduce urination at night.
This problem mostly likely is caused from a blood sugar problem.
Here’s what to do:
1. Cut out all sugars.
2. Cut out all snacks
3. Reduce the frequency of eating to 2-3 meals.
4. Add more fat to go longer without eating. Fat does not raise insulin like sugars and protein.
5. Increase potassium foods - vegetables
6. Increase the B-vitamins from nutritional yeast.
