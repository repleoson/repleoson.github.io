Leaders of the One Kenya Alliance have dismissed reports that they are seeking to form alliances with other parties ahead of 2022 elections.

Speaking in Kinamba Naivasha, the Principals say that they were united and ready to support one of their own in the elections.

Led by Wiper’s Kalonzo Musyoka and ANC’s Musalia Midavadi, they said they had not been approached by any party and will go all the way to the elections.

The leaders furthe called for unity and urged voters to carefully vet all candidates
