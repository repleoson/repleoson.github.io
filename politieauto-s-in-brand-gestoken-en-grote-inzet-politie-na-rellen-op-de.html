A sizable group of rioters, armed with rocks and other materials, ran to the police and trapped them. Police were forced to fire at the rioters. Very hopeless action, which means that fewer police cars will be available tomorrow to catch crooks and save lives! After a few hours, the situation was under control and the damage became visible. The damage to the city and the police vehicles is enormous.
