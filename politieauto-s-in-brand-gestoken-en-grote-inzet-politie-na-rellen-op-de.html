English: Riots broke out at a demonstration in the center of Rotterdam. The hundreds of demonstrators at the unannounced demonstration set off heavy fireworks and caused nuisance on the Coolsingel. Scooters were set on fire, buildings destroyed and roads barricaded. Police were on hand to monitor until a group turned on the officers. A sizable group of rioters, armed with rocks and other materials, ran to the police and trapped them. Police were forced to fire at the rioters. Two of them were hit by a police bullet and taken to hospital. A man also lost his finger for unknown reasons. When the officers were brought to safety, the rioters decided to completely destroy the abandoned police vehicles, loot them and even set one of the vehicles on fire. Very hopeless action, which means that fewer police cars will be available tomorrow to catch crooks and save lives! The fire brigade that came to extinguish the fire at one point was so much hindered and pelted with fireworks and stones that they had to flee for their lives and leave the car behind. The police arrived with many units from all over the country from Amsterdam to Limburg to expel the crowd from the center. After a few hours, the situation was under control and the damage became visible. The damage to the city and the police vehicles is enormous. Ambulances and fire engines from across the region gathered at certain secure points to stand by. Even a special fire engine from The Hague came to the scene, it has special metal grilles in front of the window to protect the windows against stones.
