English: Riots broke out at a demonstration in the center of Rotterdam. The hundreds of demonstrators at the unannounced demonstration set off heavy fireworks and caused nuisance on the Coolsingel. Scooters were set on fire, buildings destroyed and roads barricaded. Police were on hand to monitor until a group turned on the officers. A sizable group of rioters, armed with rocks and other materials, ran to the police and trapped them. Police were forced to fire at the rioters. Two of them were hit by a police bullet and taken to hospital. A man also lost his finger for unknown reasons. When the officers were brought to safety, the rioters decided to completely destroy the abandoned police vehicles, loot them and even set one of the vehicles on fire. After a few hours, the situation was under control and the damage became visible. The damage to the city and the police vehicles is enormous.
