Exercise and Count by 5 by Jack Hartmann. It’s easy to count by 5! My video, Exercise and Count by 5’s is a classroom hit across the world. It’s cool Latin beat makes it easy to exercise and count by 5.
Kids count by 5 as they exercise by stretching their arms and legs and across the mid-line of their bodies. This help kids with engaging both their bodies and their minds.
Making exercise and learning fun for kids is important for their physical and emotional growth. Exercise and Count by 5’s does this beautifully as well as develop children’s gross motor movements. This is a fun way to teach kids to count by 5.

Follow along with me and count by 5 out loud and exercise!
