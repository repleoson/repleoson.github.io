The Christian world complicates what is simple. Salvation is simple and straightforward. The Bible plainly says "Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ and you will be saved". Does this mean that believers do not sin? * Chris asks why would God cast Satan down to earth and then put his creation in the same place? * Kurt asks about ‘seek and ye shall find’. Haven’t we already found Him?
