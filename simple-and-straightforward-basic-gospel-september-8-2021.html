The Christian world complicates what is simple. Salvation is simple and straightforward. The Bible plainly says "Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ and you will be saved". When you get hold of this simple message you understand you are saved and sustained by God. * UNITED Conference - October 15-17, 2021 https://basicgospel.net/united/ * Carol asks how to know for sure she is saved. * Clay asks about Romans 8:4-5. Does this mean that believers do not sin? * Chris asks why would God cast Satan down to earth and then put his creation in the same place? * Kurt asks about ‘seek and ye shall find’. Haven’t we already found Him?
