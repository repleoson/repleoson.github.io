favourable (adjective)
Pleasing, encouraging or approving.

favourable (adjective)
Useful or helpful.

favourable (adjective)
Convenient or at a suitable time; opportune.
The rain stopped at a favourable time for our tennis match.

favourable (adjective)
Auspicious or lucky.
