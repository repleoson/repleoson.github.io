The Craftworld Eldar elite warriors. They are eldar who have given themselves to the path of Khaela Mensha Khaine, their Bloody Handed God of Murder and War. There are many forms of Aspects and each has individual style or purpose in an army. Each is drawn from the example and teachings of one of the Phoenix lords, heroic figures who emerged to lead the Eldar during the period directly after the fall.
