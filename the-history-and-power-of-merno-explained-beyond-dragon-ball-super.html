The Beyond Dragon Ball Super Universe introduces us to the fallen Angel of the once existing Universe 13, Merno but who exactly was Merno and what exactly caused Merno to be lead down this dark path of revenge and destruction? Mernos clones and the Original Merno are two very separate and different entities all together. While one is more playful and sinister about his approach, the other is ruthless and serious in how they handle each situation.
