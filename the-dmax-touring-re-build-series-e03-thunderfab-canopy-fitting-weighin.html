This episode we’re back in the ThunderFab workshop.
Everything has come back from the powder coaters and its looking so good. We remove the old canopy, bolt on the new canopy with the flares, toolboxes and light panels.

It all gets wired up, spare wheel mounted and mudflaps are fitted.

Both canopies are smoked out to replicate water/dust ingress and their both weighed - results were awesome!

The boys have built a canopy that’s stronger (engineered to handle my 110kg tinny on top), dust/water proof and yet still comes in LIGHTER than my old import.
