Kim BoKyung is singer-songwriter who became famous before debut for cover song “Because of You”, which made her ‘Korean Kelly Clarkson’. Her sensational debut with first single “Day By Day” reached #1 on all key digital charts and her sorrowful and powerful vocal style appeals to many Korean fans. She released single “Suddenly” for drama “City Hunter” OST.
