Drawing on insights from Paramahansa Yogananda’s teachings, Self-Realization Fellowship nun Sister Preeti explains how our human relationships can be elevated and spiritualized when we are operating through compassion and understanding.

Sister Preeti shares stories to illustrate how we can learn to participate in all our relationships with respect and love, reaching a state of spiritual balance which is central to Paramahansaji’s “how-to-live” teachings.
