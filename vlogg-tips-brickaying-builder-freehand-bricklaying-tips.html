Now Traditional Bricklaying Everyday breeds good practice and good practice Breeds Permanent.
So working traditionally Everyday enables me to build 4-6 courses freehand and I’l still be level , plumb and gauge (unless I’v been out the night before 😉)

This is a method I will pass onto my sons and every Learning trowel that follows me on all social platforms 👌

Whatever you’re method be good , be clean and be honest 🧱🏠
