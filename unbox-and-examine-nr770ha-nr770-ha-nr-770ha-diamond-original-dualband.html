This antenna is 40.2 inches (1.02 m) high. The lower coil and antenna are silver. The connector type is UHF (for PO mount). This antenna does not include the mount or coax.
his antenna design employs DC ground structure so that the whip element and ground section (outer shell of impedance matching section) are short circuited, and the center conductor of the feed point connector and whip element are isolated.
This antenna features a tiltable whip structure. Chrome colored.
