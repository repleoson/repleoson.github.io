First you’ll learn what the expression "not only X, but also Y" means. We use this expression to identify two characteristics, traits or points. It’s important to note that these two must match. So they’re either BOTH positive or BOTH negative.

Next we’ll look at some example sentences using "not only X, but also Y". We’ll examine the English sentence structure so you can understand the advanced English grammar.
