CF.TV Agnes Zee and Marissa Higgins held some great interview during the presentations TEN @The Spoke Club to share with views at home. The presentation give us a great opportunities to learn more about Ten@The Spoke club, the designers and they design.

On a Friday night mid November was proving to be a winning combination for pop-up organizer , Tamika Auwai. TEN@TheSpokeClub,held on Friday, November 16th combines ten local retailers under one roof, members-only club (The Spoke Club).
Guests shop from various of brands, including: Calisto Designs, Copious Couture, Fredrick Prince Jewellery, Headmistress, Jordan de Ruiter, Loft 604, Psalms 91:1, Scarfparty, Sentaler and Tetiana K.

What a great way for a group of Toronto designers to around up the year 2012 in style.
