English in use:
How are you? I’m doing fine, thank you.
What’s going on? I got a teddy bear.
Are you okay? Not really.
Do you need anything? No thanks.
How have you been? I’ve been a little bored.
What’s up? I’m taking a break.
You look upset. I’m a little worried.
Can I help you in any way? I’m looking for a market.
Expressions:
Thanks / It’s my pleasure. / Wait up. / Where can it be? / Where to? / Take these. / What’s up? / Later.
