In part 4 of our series, Questions about the Quran, Dr. Shabir Ally says there are 3 major themes: 1) belief in God, 2) belief in the message that has come from God, and 3) the belief in the life hereafter. He points out that our responsibilities as human beings to God and humanity is a prominent feature in the Quran.
