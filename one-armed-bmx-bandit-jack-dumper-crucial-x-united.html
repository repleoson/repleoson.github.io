There’s nothing much purer in BMX than a young talent thriving on the joys of bike riding, and that is only topped by determined minds such as Bristol, UK’s Jack Dumper. Enjoy a short but sweet video with Crucial BMX / United rider Jack tearing up his local Campus Skatepark in Bristol.
