Are you still trying to work like this?
Frustrating isn’t it but what is the solution?

Cloud hosted phone systems using the latest VoIP technology give you and your team absolute freedom and flexibility, they enable you to work remotely, you can integrate multiple sites and locations, easily

So that sounds great but will this new technology cost you more?

No, having a VoIP system eliminates the need for physical phone lines, you can make unlimited calls at any one time without paying for additional lines, which can significantly reduce costs and save time too.

Businesses with VoIP save on average 62% on their communication costs.
