Are you still trying to work like this?
Frustrating isn’t it but what is the solution?

Cloud hosted phone systems using the latest VoIP technology give you and your team absolute freedom and flexibility, they enable you to work remotely, you can integrate multiple sites and locations, easily
scale capacity and access all of your information across multiple devices. All you need is an internet connection.

So that sounds great but will this new technology cost you more?

No, having a VoIP system eliminates the need for physical phone lines, you can make unlimited calls at any one time without paying for additional lines, which can significantly reduce costs and save time too.

Businesses with VoIP save on average 62% on their communication costs.

Hosted phone systems work for a variety of traffic levels and are feature rich with interactive menus, voicemail, voicemail to email, directories and call recording, you can even remotely monitor all phone activity through a dedicated portal.

Why waste hours trying to fix any issues yourself when you could have a team of fully qualified manufacturer trained engineers on hand to support and maintain your devices?
Why not invest in a service that is easily scalable as your business grows and can integrate multiple sites and locations and easily scale capacity as needed?

BT are already phasing out traditional phone lines, this is the future.
