He studied at the School of Medicine, in the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. Today, he works at the Psychiatric Hospital of Attica as a Specialist Psychiatrist. His main subject of scientific interest is Sexology and he has been involved in the study and clinical treatment of transsexualism issues since 2006.
