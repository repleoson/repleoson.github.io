The band was formed in 2004 and in 2007 they began posting their videos on YouTube to great success. To date, the band has over 4.7 Billion YouTube views and over 13 Million subscribers to their channel, making theirs one of the most frequented worldwide. The band has performed headline shows in more than 30 countries all over the world, and continue to tour new places every year.
