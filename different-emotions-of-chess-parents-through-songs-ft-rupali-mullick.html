Rupali Mullick is a woman with many accomplishments. She is a Ph.D in chemical engineering from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and is a partner at JVM Industries. Rupali travels with Raahil to different tournaments and in this video she expresses the feelings of a parent when their child wins or losses games in the form of different songs!
