SAMAJHNA, MANANA

ENGLISH USE:
OPINION
…………………………………………..
EX:
Main tumhe honest samajhta hun.
Wah mujhe pagal samajhti hai.

STRUCTURE:
[Sub + consider/considers/considered + him/her….. + to be + ob]

Ex: I consider you to be honest.
She considers me to be mad.
