Emerging as a BC Anchor is a huge accomplishment with wide-ranging benefits to the economy and ecosystem. The finalists are: AbCellera, Absolute Software, Cymax Group Technologies and WELL Health.

The Company of the Year – Anchor award is presented in partnership with Deloitte.
