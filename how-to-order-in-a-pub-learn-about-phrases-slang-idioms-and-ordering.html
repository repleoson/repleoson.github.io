In this lesson you’ll learn how to order in a pub, and also how to sound like a real local while you’re in there.
Pubs are incredibly popular in the UK, and traditional pubs still offer a glimpse of true British culture. Maybe you’ve been out sightseeing all day and you’re looking for a nice place to relax. Learn how to communicate in a pub with this pub English lesson.
