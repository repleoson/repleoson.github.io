Various Avatars Of Lord Shiva

To sustain the law and balance of nature or existence, Lord Shiva took many avatars in various yugas. Kurma Purana talks about 28 avatars of Shiva but in Shiva Purana, the following 19 Avatars of Shiva are mentioned.

Pippalada Avatar
Nandi Avatar
Veerbhadra Avatar
Sharabha Avatar
Ashwathhama
Bhairava Avatar
Durvasa Avatar

Vrishabha Avatar
Yatinath Avatar
Krishna Darshan avatar
Bhikshuvarya Avatar
Sureshwar Avatar
Keerat Avatar
Suntantarka Avatar
Brahmachari Avatar
Yaksheshwar Avatar
Avadhoot Avatar
