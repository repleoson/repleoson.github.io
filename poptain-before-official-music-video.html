Poptain releases his a new single "Before" a melodic song which pays homage to the sounds that put Zimbabwean music on the map. In this love song Poptain tells a story about a women has been in many unsuccessful relationships and is looking for something new and fresh, the guy in the story is an old friend who has always loved her but knows he won’t be able to tell her anything different from before so he will have to prove it with his actions. On Before, Poptain worked with Mega-T who was one of the very first producers to recognize his musical gifts and help develop his sound and style, this song serves as a sweet reminder to the times before. The visuals, shot by SIMDOC who brought a unique angle to the video having Poptain come across in a different light.
