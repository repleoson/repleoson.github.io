The Mom and Dads were a Western-styled folk music group from Spokane, Washington that specialised in waltzes, polkas, and general easy listening. The quartet featured Doris A. Crow (June 17, 1905 – September 28, 1998) on piano, Quentin Ratliff (b. 1934) on saxophone, Leslie Welch (February 2, 1912 – February 1, 1983) on accordion, and Harold Hendren (July 12, 1919 - September 9, 2008) on drums.
