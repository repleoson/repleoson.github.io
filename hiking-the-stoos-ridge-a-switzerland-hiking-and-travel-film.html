This video has subtitles for the talky parts :) So… Switzerland 2! Guten Tag everybody. I hope you’re all doing well and that you agree with me that this is one of the most beautiful spots on Earth!

I had a lav mic on for the end part, but it didn’t work for some reason, so it’s only camera audio… I hope you can hear it, otherwise I’ve put subtitles / closed captions that you can switch on on the video player. And I will try my best to avoid tech problems in the future :)

Thanks so much for the reaction to Switzerland 1. Love ya

This is me hiking the Stoos ridge, which is probably the best day hike from Zurich, or even the best day trip from Zurich. I got the bus to the Schwyz Stoosbahn and went up the Stoos furnicular, before riding the chairlift up to the start of the Stoos ridge hike. I hope you enjoy this hiking film and travel guide :)
