According to Shaquille O’Neal, LeBron James doesn’t have to win another title to become the GOAT. All the King simply has to do is pass Kareem Abdul-Jabbar as the NBA’s all-time leading scorer to go with the four championships he’s already got, and Shaq said he’ll be cemented as quote ‘the best player ever.’ Skip Bayless and Shannon Sharpe discuss how much they believe getting the record will help convince doubters that LeBron is the GOAT.
