In the Sermon on the Mount, did Jesus teach that having lust in the heart is as sinful as engaging in adultery and that harboring anger is as sinful as committing murder? Sproul reminds us of the nature of God’s justice and the perfect way in which the Lord measures our sin against His holy law.
