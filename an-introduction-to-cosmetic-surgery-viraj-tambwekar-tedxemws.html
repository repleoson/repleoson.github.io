Dr. Viraj Tambwekar is a highly qualified and experienced cosmetic surgeon, which is why speaking about plastic surgery and the negative connotations that accompany it comes with his job. In his talk, he explores the misconceptions surrounding plastic surgery, and the importance of recognizing it as a personal choice - a choice that every individual is entitled to. A choice that grants confidence, self-empowerment, and gratification. Viraj Tambwekar is a qualified plastic surgeon that has worked in the field of surgery for many years. He is highly accomplished having many achievements under his belt and recognition from various platforms. Today, he plans to talk about the evolution of trends in plastic surgery and hopes to end the stigma surrounding cosmetic surgery here in India.
