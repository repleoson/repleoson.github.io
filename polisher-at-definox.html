The shiny appearance of the valves, a true signature of the products manufactured by Definox, contributes to our brand image. The level of finish provides good in-line cleanability and compliance with IAA sanitary standards and requirements. The mastery of the specific actions of the polisher profession is the guarantee of proven and optimal quality.
