You are watching the official video for "It’s Going To Happen!" by the Undertones.

A typically energetic performance from Derry’s finest on this top 20 single from 1981. The ambitious video recreates a silent movie filming set complete with the kind of bad puns that showcase the band’s childlike sense of humour. The song though is a dark tale of friends `too slow to notice what’s wrong’.

The video has been remastered to HD to celebrate the 40th anniversary of its original release on 21st April 1981.
