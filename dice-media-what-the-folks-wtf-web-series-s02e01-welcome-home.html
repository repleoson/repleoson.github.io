In the first season of What The Folks!, we saw the Sharma family warm up to their son-in-law, Nikhil Solanki and form an everlasting bond.

This time around, Dice Media takes you to Nikhil’s side of the family - the affable Solankis - as Anita now tries to be a part of this crazy family!

Dice Media and Epigamia present What The Folks! Season 2, powered by Pepperfry, in partnership with Google.

Episode 1

A few months after the events of the first season, Nikhil and Anita are settled in Mumbai.
Nikhil’s parents, Kiran and Vandana Solanki, decide to pay them a short visit to see how they are doing. This triggers Anita - ever-the-perfectionist - to make sure everything is in order. But awkwardness abounds when some minor misunderstanding leads to a comedy of errors.
Will Anita be able to warm up to her in-laws in the same way Nikhil did to her parents? Will Kiran and Vandana be as ‘chill’ as the Sharmas?
Keep watching as this crazy roller-coaster ride enters its second season, and welcome to the What The Folks family!
