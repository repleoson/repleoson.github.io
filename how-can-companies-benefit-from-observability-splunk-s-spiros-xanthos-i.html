Observability – what is it? Until now, the tools IT and DevOps teams have relied on to monitor and manage applications have often been disconnected. Jo Peterson and Spiros Xanthos, Vice President of Project Management, Observability and IT Operations at Splunk, do a deep dive on Observability discussing the benefits and added value organizations receive from implementing this full stack approach.
