Defence Minister Peter Dutton would “make an excellent leader” of the Liberal Party in the future, according to Liberal Senator Alex Antic.

Mr Dutton has told The Australian he wouldn’t rule out attempting a run for the Prime Ministership should Scott Morrison retire.

“He is a very, very good bloke – he is a man of principle and a strong leader,” Mr Antic said.

“He would make an excellent leader when the time comes.”
