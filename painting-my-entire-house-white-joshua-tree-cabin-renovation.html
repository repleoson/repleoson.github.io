On today’s episode of Modern Builds I’ll be showing you how to paint a stucco house with an airless sprayer. I shared as many painting tips and tricks as I’ve got, along with a few of my mistakes to learn from along the way. As a design concept I decided to go completely monochromatic and "color block" the house flat white. This is pretty common in commercial and high end architecture but stands out a little bit in some more traditional neighborhoods… I like that!
