This is the absolutely insane Zenvo TSR-S!

It’s an ultra-rare Danish hypercar, and the car you see in this video is just one of fifteen ever made, and each one will cost you an eye-watering €1.5m - and that’s before tax!

So what do you get for your money? Well for starters, the Zenvo is one of the most eye-catching road-legal cars around! There’s a whole shed load of aero across the entire car, from huge air intakes up front through to a humungous wing at the back!

When it comes to performance, the TSR-S is powered by an insane engine - a 5.8-litre twin-supercharged V8 that puts down 1200hp & 1200Nm of torque! It’ll also hit 60mph in 2.9 seconds and has a top speed of over 200mph!

So it’s eye-catching, but is it really worth the huge price tag? You’ll need to keep watching to find out!
