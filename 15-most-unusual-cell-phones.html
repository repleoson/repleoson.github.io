In the past 4 decades, cell phones have come a long way. Once large, bulky, and only affordable for wealthy businessmen, they’re now a vital part of everyday life for just about everyone. The route to this point has been a varied one, though, with incredible innovation and technological advances… but for every design that moved things in the right direction, there were plenty that went the wrong way. From strange shapes, to unexpected features, here are the top 15 unusual cell phones ever made.
