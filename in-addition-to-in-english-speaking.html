After watching this video you will be able to understand the meaning and proper use of an advance English structure "IN ADDITION AND IN ADDITION TO"
………………………………………………………………………………………………….
HINDI PEHCHAN:
Ke alawa, ke atirikt

ENGLISH SITUATION:
BESIDES,
……………………………………………………………………………………………….

Ex:
In addition to cricket, he is good at basketball.
Cricket ke alawa wah basket ball me bhi achcha hai.

He loves his wife. In addition, he spends time with her and takes her for shopping.
Wah apni patni se pyar karta hai. Iske alawa wah use shopping ke liye le jata hai.
