This video opens with Khen Rinpoche Geshe Thubten Chonyi, the abbot of Kopan Monastery, giving an update of the activities at the Kopan during the lockdown.

Lama Zopa Rinpoche begins his teaching, recorded on April 7, 2020, at Kopan, again emphasizing the importance of bodhichitta.

“There are many eminent teachings, qualities of the mind, realizations, but, ‘Among those eminences, bodhichitta, the supreme thought of enlightenment, is the most eminent,’” Rinpoche says, referring to a quote from Khunu Lama Rinpoche’s Praise to Bodhichitta.

Rinpoche also gives the lung for the Five Powerful Mantras.
