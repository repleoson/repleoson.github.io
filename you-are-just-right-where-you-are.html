We grew up with the sayings, ‘New friends are silver, old friends are gold’ and, ‘Friends are there when you need them’.
Sometimes your best friends are those who are not there when you need them. Because then you have to go beyond need and you will find something much more profound than the comfort your mind seeks.
The most powerful things that happens sometime comes with much pain.
You know by your own insight that you are just right where you are.
