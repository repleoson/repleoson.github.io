This short video provides an overview of the standard methods used in Pipe & Cable locating. It will prepare you to effectively use pipe and cable locators and enhance your ability to determine target location, target depth, and the tracing distance. If you’re a beginner, this training video will help you get up and running quickly. If you’re an experience locator, this video will enhance your knowledge and provide you with an in-depth view of how the locating process really works.
