2020 was Europe’s hottest year on record ‘by a considerable amount, ‘ scientists sayThe UK Met Office, which contributed to the report, said record had been broken "by a considerable amount." Average temperatures in Europe last year were 1.9 degrees Celsius above the 1981-2010 average, a period used as a benchmark to measure changing temperatures.The five warmest years in Europe have all been since 2014.The findings were laid out in the American Meteorological Society’s 31st State of the Climate
