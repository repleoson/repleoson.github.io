{% raw %}Universal Health Coverage (UHC) aims to ensure everyone receives the quality services they need, and is protected from health threats, without suffering financial hardship. It's a way of reducing poverty and increasing health security, so that no-one is left behind. This video message in support of UHC was recorded by WHO Director-General Margaret Chan for UHC Day 2015.<br /><br />For more information, visit <br /><br /><a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="http://www.who.int/universal_health_coverage.">http://www.who.int/universal_health_coverage.</a>{% endraw %}
