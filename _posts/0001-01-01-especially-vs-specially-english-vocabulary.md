{% raw %}What is the difference between especially and specially in English?<br /><br />&quot;especially&quot; and &quot;specially&quot; are both adverbs.<br /><br />We use &quot;especially&quot; to describe one person, thing, or situation that is above all the others.<br />The meaning is similar to &quot;in particular&quot;<br />Example: I like all of my friends, especially Mark.<br /><br />&quot;specially&quot; on the other hand describes the specific (particular) purpose of something.<br />Example: The bag was specially designed for my computer.<br /> <br />There are many more examples and explanations in the video.
