An imperative is a verb in the base form.<br /> <br />Common uses of the imperative are as follows:<br />Orders and instructions.<br />to encourage someone.<br />to suggest something.<br />to offer something.<br />to express wishes for someone's welfare.<br /><br />For detailed examples of the above, watch the video.<br /><br />We can also express first person imperatives with
