{% raw %}What is Precis?<br />A precis is a summary of the essentials of a text.<br />It is the gist or main theme of a passage expressed in as few words as possible preferably 1/3rd of the original text.<br /><br />It must have three C's in it.<br /><br />1. Clear<br />2. Concise <br />3. Complete<br /><br />Why Precis is required?<br /><br />1. Precis-writing is an excellent exercise in reading. <br />Precis-writing forces you to pay attention to what you read; So our attention gets improved a lot.<br /><br />2. Precis-writing is also a very good exercise in writing a composition.<br />Most of us repeat ourselves a lot while writing and unnecessarily exaggerate the content. <br />but Precis-writing teaches one how to express one's thoughts clearly, concisely, and effectively.<br /><br /><br />The six steps of precis writing are :<br />1. reading<br />2. Analyze<br />3. Provide a suitable title<br />4. Initial Draft<br />5. Revise and Edit<br />6.
