{% raw %}“seem” is a regular verb in English. <br /><br />&quot;seem&quot; is a linking verb (also called a copular verb). Linking verbs are followed by adjectives (not adverbs). Linking verbs can also be followed by noun phrases. A linking verb gives us more information about the subject.<br /><br />In this lesson, you will learn the meaning and uses of &quot;seem&quot;:<br /><br />&quot;seem&quot; as a linking verb (seem + adjective and seem to be + adjective)<br />&quot;it seems&quot;
