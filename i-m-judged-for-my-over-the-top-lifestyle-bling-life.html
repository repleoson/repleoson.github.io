MEET Staci Doll - a businesswoman and influencer who loves her over the top lifestyle. Staci, 30, of Toronto, Canada, has not always lived a life of luxury but began earning decent money from the age of 18 through dancing. Staci said she used to be very frugal but that her first major purchases were investing in herself and her body. To date, she’s spent over $100,000 on cosmetic surgery and procedures. Staci now runs an online boutique and said she has felt judged due to where she started out but asserts she has earned this luxury through hard work. She said: “People assume that I’m a gold digger but I made the money - very hard earned money - and got this lifestyle.” Staci’s lavish lifestyle is evident from her custom-made furniture to jewellery and she loves being able to buy what she wants… Including a second luxury car.
