Addiction and resentment often coincide. Many of the people we’ve seen struggle with addiction have resentment because they are always worried that no one respects them or likes them. This causes them to go out of their way and do things for other people they wouldn’t normally do. Also known as people pleasing. This often leads to resentment and anger.
