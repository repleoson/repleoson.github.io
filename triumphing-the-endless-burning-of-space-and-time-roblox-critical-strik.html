This event was made to remember Roblox’s major outage a week ago. It’s a pretty difficult event map too with many flame enemies and unique mechanics. He is very similar to Ignitus from Tower Blitz in lots of ways! Anyways, thanks to Leeelooo, Grand, and Cosmo for helping me beat this event and complete everything in the game so far.
