Before the Dawn:

Before the Dawn is turn-based Medieval Zombie Apocalypse created by an Indie Game company named Time Stone Games, where the player aims to survive from incoming zombies by collecting weapons, foods, medical utilities, and etc.

Before the Dawn is different from any other zombie apocalypse games. Its setting is based on the Medieval age, which makes the game a bit harder since weapon technology is not as developed as the 21st century. Lack of high-tech weapons, therefore, lead the players to fear. Because of these reasons, players have to avoid the zombies to get to the final destination, The Sanctuary.

There are no other games like Before the Dawn. One of the advantages is that the game is based on the medieval age without any magic or fantasy. If magics and powerful heroes become a common part of the game, it would ruin the whole purpose of the survival game.
