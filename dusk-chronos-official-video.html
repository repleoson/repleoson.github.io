"Chronos" is the second single from DUSK ‘s upcoming themed album , “ ODYSSEY ”, which is due to be released in January 2019. Following the first single “Yenesis” , “Chronos” debates the relationship between mankind & the passing of time. A relationship that can sometimes be arrogant & ignorant and at other times can be suffocatingly stressful .It is yet unknown if the one having awareness of the limited time they’re given is lucky or deeply unfortunate but the only thing that is for certain is that they will only be completely free when it ends. With a kind of love letter DUSK invites people to coexist, empathize & share the feeling & the beauty of Now & “ Us ” , leaving behind for a while the autistic realization of their Ego.
