C-Trak offer a range of #accumulation #conveyors to sort your products and #accumulate them. This project is for sorting bearings using a modular belt system. It is constructed in stainless steel using plastic chain. It comes supplied with variable speeds and will be incorporated into the customer own equipment on site.
