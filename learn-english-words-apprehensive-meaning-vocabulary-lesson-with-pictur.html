What does Apprehensive mean? Learn the meaning of Apprehensive as we define this advanced vocabulary word with a simple definition, pictures, example sentences, English pronunciation and audio.

Apprehensive definition: worried that something bad may happen; afraid

Examples of Apprehensive in a sentence:

1. Terry, a brave and willing man, is the least apprehensive of danger.

2. With recent job cuts, Kate is apprehensive about losing her job.

3. Apprehensive about the consequences of texting and driving, Jill turned her phone off.

4. After a few swimming lessons, she was less apprehensive about swimming.

5. The apprehensive parents kept their children indoors as neighborhood crime increased.
