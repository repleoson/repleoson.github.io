The banking system is functioning as a, "crime syndicate," says Gerald Celente, Trends Journal Founder. "This economy [U.S.] is going way down, and prices are going way up," he alerts viewers. Celente believes that the crypto market will continue to rise in price until the governments stops it.

"I believe in gold more than anything," he says as a way to prepare for possible turbulence in markets as inflation rises. "We are living under conditions that you could have never invented in January, 2020," Celente concludes.
