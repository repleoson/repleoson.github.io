7-year-old Anay wanted to surprise his mother for her birthday with a handmade greeting card. Seeing the card, Anay’s father realized that they could personalize it further - by converting his drawing into wearable jewelry for his mother. A simple butterfly drawing was turned into a pendant by Imaginarium Precious, a gift that Anay’s mother now holds very close to her heart.
