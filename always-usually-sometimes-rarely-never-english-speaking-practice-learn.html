More Practice:https://youtu.be/azjFfCn2VF0 KeyWords: always, usually, sometimes, rarely and never. these adverbs of frequency will help you speak more naturally. These adverbs are placed in everyday situations that take place in the afternoon. Each phrase has a picture and illustration for better understanding.
