Ever wondered how to write an interesting character? There are lots of questions we can ask ourselves in a character questionnaire to get a really good feel of who our characters are. Surface-level questions, such as what they look like and what their name is. But also deeper questions. In this video, I offer a character questionnaire that explores some of those deeper questions – ones designed to help us tell a better story. This is part of a series of writing tips called 21 Questions.
