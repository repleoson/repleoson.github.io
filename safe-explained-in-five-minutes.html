This video explains SAFe 5.0 in five minutes (and 29 seconds). About Scaled Agile, Inc.
The Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe®), created by Scaled Agile, Inc., is the #1 framework for business agility, with proven benefits in productivity, time-to-market, quality, and employee engagement.
