contrary (adjective)
Opposite; in an opposite direction; in opposition; adverse.

contrary (adjective)
Opposed; contradictory; inconsistent.

contrary (adjective)
Given to opposition; perverse; wayward.
A contrary disposition; a contrary child.

contrary (adverb)
Contrarily.

contrary (noun)
