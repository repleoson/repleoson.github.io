As someone for a least a short while who has limited mobility in my right arm i have been struggling to do even the simplest of tasks. One of those is lacing up my shoes! Impossible! You cant get a good bow tied with one hand.

As such i decided to look into running shoes which are accessible. Meaning are you able to lace them up with one hand. Sadly the choice is extremely limited. I have covered some of the main options today but if i have missed anything let me know in the comments below.

Featured in this video are the Nike Air Zoom Pegasus 38 Flyease and the Tempo Next% Flyease along with the Boa lacing system and the Salomon Quicklace tech too.
