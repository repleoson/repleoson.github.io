This major update to Trapcode Particular features the long-requested ability to work with Particular and Form in the same 3D space by bringing Form systems to Particular!

The release also sees upgrades to the Flocking simulation with the addition of On Predator/Prey Contact and Team designations as well as improvements to system organizational capabilities in the Designer.
