Available in PNG shops this week is the locally produced dairy fresh milk.

Ilimo Dairy Farm, at 14 Mile outside Port Moresby, has 500 cows producing 6 tonnes of fresh milk every day.

Executive Director of Innovative Agro Industry, Ilan Weiss, stated that PNG is now able to produce one third of the country’s dairy imports.
