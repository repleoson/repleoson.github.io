Going Medieval continues and also concludes as we finally reach the end of the research tree to unlock all the secrets and technology that a medieval peasant could ever want or even dream of!

Alongside the apothecary table we also build a distillery to make some purest alcohol, which is another ingredient for the fancier healing kits.
