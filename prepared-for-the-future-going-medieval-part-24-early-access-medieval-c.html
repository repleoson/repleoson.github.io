Going Medieval continues and also concludes as we finally reach the end of the research tree to unlock all the secrets and technology that a medieval peasant could ever want or even dream of!

Using our new research we build ourselves an apothecary‚Äôs table, which allows us to mix herbs and other things to make proper healing kits! We begin with some basic ones with the goal of making some more extravagant ones, but herbs are hard to come by in winter. Alongside the apothecary table we also build a distillery to make some purest alcohol, which is another ingredient for the fancier healing kits.

The traumatic trail is upgraded with some proper, shiny, painful looking metal traps, and they soon show their effectiveness when we get attacked. Also during this attack we see the appearance of the amazing teleporting baddie!
