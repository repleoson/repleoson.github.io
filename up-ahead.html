Okay, I’m going to go ahead and start packing.

I’m way ahead of you. I’ve already put together a list of candidates.
(직역: 난 당신을 앞서가고 있다)

" No matter what I do, she’s always one step ahead of me."
내가 어떻게 하든, 그녀는 나보다 항상 한발 앞서있어.

The criminal turned out to be a former policeman. So, he was very familiar with their investigative methods. That’s how he was able to always stay one step ahead of the police and avoid being captured for almost a year.

" The company has managed to stay ahead of its competitors for more than a decade."
그 회사는 10년 넘도록 경쟁사들보다 앞선 위치를 유지할 수 있었다.
