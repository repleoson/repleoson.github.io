For this routine, I used classic exercises and made them a bit „easier“.
= Squats + Tippie Toes instead of Jump Squats
= Plank Jump + Walking Jack instead of Plank Jump + Jumping Jack, etc. :)

Therefore, I douuuubt you will feel like calling me bad names during those 10min :D Plus, the view & the music are great!

PS: I filmed this video fully on my own!
Camera, tripod, iPhone, timers, exercises, smiling, sunset, hair - that was multitasking at its finest :D



▸ calories burned are between 70-110kcal, depending on your weight, height & fitness level! I’m a short and tiny person & I burned 70kcal :)

The video is in full length which means you can just follow whatever I’m doing 30s for each exercise. If you need a break - TAKE IT! Don’t worry too much about that. You will improve over time :) that’s the best feeling!
