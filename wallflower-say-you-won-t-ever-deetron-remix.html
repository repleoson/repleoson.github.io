An eclectic demonstration of vocal and song-writing prowess, Wallflower is a collaborative project between Alison Marks and Leah Floyeurs. Having made their name as separate performers, Wallflower sees these two English artists come together to deliver the ‘Say You Won’t Ever EP’ on Defected Records, featuring remixes from Deetron and Asadinho.

The Deetron Remix is based around an elevating synth arpeggio, which creates an ethereal backdrop for the hair-raising vocals.
