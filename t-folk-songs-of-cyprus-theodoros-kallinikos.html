Theodoulos Kallinikos was the first ever person to record and release traditional songs of Cyprus back in the 1930’s (in Athens) on 78 rpm discs. From 1924-1948 he visited almost the whole of Cyprus and collected, under adverse conditions many folk songs of the island and brought them to the surface. Thank’s to Kallinikos many of these songs have survived. Here is a reproduction of the original recordings done for the first time on cd disc.
