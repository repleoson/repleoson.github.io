Shawn kicks off the 167 series with an honest and vulnerable update on his battle with anxiety and the lessons he’s learned about the “necessary endings” we all encounter in our lives. This message is truly one of Shawn’s finest, and it can deeply impact each of us as we work through our past pain and trauma, as we allow God to heal old wounds, and as we accept God’s love for us even in our lowest seasons.
