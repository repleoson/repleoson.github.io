Good architecture does not shout. It doesn’t do this cabin either. With the simple building body, the simple details and the light, slim roof, Koia gets a quiet appearance in the surroundings. The narrow horizontal drawbar and the continuous window and slab panels emphasize the tight architecture. The entrance area is recessed, as is the terrace in the gable.
Hytta’s floor plan is neat and clear. It contains two bedrooms, bathroom, storage room, porch and open living / kitchen area. The ceiling of 30 degrees also provides space for a spacious storage or lodging home. Large windows let in a lot of light, and the open ceiling in the living room and kitchen increases the feeling of space.
Koia Moderne has identical floor plan as Koia Tradisjon borset from the home solution. Here you can see how the important details determine which character the cottage gets.
