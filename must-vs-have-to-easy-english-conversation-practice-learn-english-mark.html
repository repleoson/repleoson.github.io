Learn English - Mark Kulek ESL. Use Must for personal duties and Have To for obligations. More Practice: https://youtu.be/M6zvuKb4ChU Everyday activities - going to the bookstore, going to the shopping mall, doing laundry, cleaning the bathroom, watching golf, exercising, gong to the movies, going to the supermarket, washing a car, going to a coffee shop, feed the dog. These patterns are color coded so that you can see how the sentences are constructed and will be easier for you to learn. There is also reading practice in this video. This is an interactive listening and speaking video. Have fun learning about your preferences and have fun speaking English now.
Language in this video:
Do you want to go to the bookstore? Sorry, I can’t. I must do my laundry today.
Do you wan to go to the shopping mall? Sorry, I can’t. I must clean the bathroom today.
Do you want to watch golf? Sorry, I can’t. I must exercise today.
Do you want to go to the movies? Sorry, I can’t. I have to go to the supermarket today.
Do you want to go to the park? Sorry, I can’t. I have to wash my wife’s car today.
Do you want to go to the coffee shop? Sorry, I can’t. I have to feed the dog today.
