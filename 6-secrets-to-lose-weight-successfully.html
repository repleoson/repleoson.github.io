SUMMARY:
- Change your eating habits step by step, with no rush. Take small but healthy steps on a daily basis and make slow but steady progress.
- You should weigh yourself daily and register all the changes. Remember that slow and steady progress is the ideal way to lose weight and to improve your habits without too much suffering.
- A healthy diet plan does help you lose weight, but you should combine proper nutrition with physical exercise. Even low-risk activities, such as walking will be helpful.
- The moral support is critical when you’re on a diet. Your friends know that when you do give in to temptation, you’ll feel guilty afterward. Therefore, they can remind you to be careful with high-calorie foods when they are with you.
- Before giving up, learn more about your metabolism. If you have a slow metabolism, arm yourself with patience and don’t let your guard down. Remember that long-term results become visible one day.
- If you decided to start a diet, contact a good nutritionist for supervision and advice. Let the professional come up with a food plan according to your needs and the capabilities of your body.
