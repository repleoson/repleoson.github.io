If you have feelings for someone besides your soulmate you and your partner will feel intense pain, forcing you two to separate. After Kace and Heather receive their sign, they realised they weren’t destined to be. Heather wanted the best for Kace so she let him go even though she didn’t want too. Lena tries to be strong and patient with Kace knowing they are meant to be even though Kace is stubborn. Heather wants to find her soulmate and pays for a procedure, but finds out that she doesn’t have one…After more problems arise Kace was able to personally meet Goddess Aeverie and begs her to change the signs. Having Heather on earth was going to destroy not only Kaces future but others. He finally realised that nothing would work and Heather realised that she wasn’t meant to be on earth in the first place. Everything went back to normal and Heather turns into a goddess.
