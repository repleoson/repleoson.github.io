In this tutorial video we will show you how to make a T-Top replacement cover. Sailrite® has the supplies and tools that you need for this project and more. We will be using Stamoid™ Top which is a vinyl fabric that is completely waterproof and considered a premium fabric that is often used for Mega Yachts.
Sailrite carries many different brands of fabric’s that make great T-Tops. Brands like Stamoid™ Top, Sunbrella®, Top Notch® 9, Herculite®, Weblon® Regatta® & Top Gun® and more.
