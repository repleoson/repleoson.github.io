Alberta Premier Jason Kenney is facing growing pressure to resign over his role in the province’s COVID-19 crisis. Is a leadership review coming, and how would that impact Alberta as it fights the fourth wave? Heather Yourex-West reports.

Habiba Sarabi, who once served as Afghanistan’s first female governor, fears that the Taliban will once again push women out of society. Crystal Goomansingh explains why.
