- Method 1: Uses INDEX & AGGREGATE formulas. This doesn’t require CSE (control shift enter) and it works on older Excel versions.
- Method 2: Uses the relatively new TEXTJOIN formula (available in Excel Office 365, Excel 2019) and requires CSE.
