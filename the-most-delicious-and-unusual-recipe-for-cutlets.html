Unusual Cutlets Recipe
Minced meat-1kg
Breadcrumbs-1 pack
Eggs-1pc
Onion-1pc (medium)
Dill-1 bunch
Parsley-1 bunch
Dried garlic-2h / l
Salt-2 h / l (to taste)
Black pepper-2h / l (to taste)
Vegetable oil for frying
Sour cream-3st / l
Mayonnaise-1 st / l
