SIMPLY GENIUS UPCYCLING IDEAS

You will be surprised but ice-cube trays are very useful items and could be used in various ways. Most of using them to make ice but you can also make sweets, bath bombs, candles, and even sushi! Watch this video to find these simple but genius ideas:
-You can remove unwanted odors from cutting boards with one ingredient. Freeze toothpaste in an ice-cube tray and use to clean a cutting board
-If you like bath bombs, make them at home and save a lot of money. You can make various forms and colors. We share a couple of cool recipes! In a bowl, combine baking soda, citric acid, coconut oil, essential oil, and coloring
-Pour melted chocolate into an ice-cube tray, ad berries you like most and let harden. Delicious sweets are ready!
-You can make homemade candles. Grate crayons and place them into an ice-cube tray, cover with candle wax. Place candlewick inside and let harden
-You can make cube sushi in a silicone mold! Same taste but less time and supplies! Add a layer of avocado, shrimps, fish or any topping you like! Spread rice on top of fish and put it into the refrigerator for 20 minutes. Flip tray onto a cutting board and enjoy
-Do you love jewelry making? Find out a cool tutorial on how to make cute earrings
-You can use an ice tray as a seed starter
-Fill an ice cube tray with mint leaves and lime juice. Freeze. You can add these cubes to your drinks
