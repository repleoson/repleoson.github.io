‘What The Folks’ is a journey of how modern families are breaking stereotypes, overcoming generation gaps, and growing to love one another, despite having starkly different world views. The show encapsulates the familial struggles of Nikhil, a young man who is forced to live with his in-laws for a few weeks.

Episode 3:
After all the awkwardness in the Sharma household, happy times are finally here. Anita has come home for Sudha and Prakash’s anniversary and the family spends a fun day together. However, during the Sharma anniversary party with relatives, several relatives ask umpteen uncomfortable questions. Will Nikhil be able to control himself?
