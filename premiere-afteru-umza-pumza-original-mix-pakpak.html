Release Info:
PakPak Records welcome AfterU for a new two track EP that takes you on a heady and spiritual journey. This pair is made up of Elmar Ivatarov & Vitali Pevzner and they bring a fine mix of melodic techno and deep house to their sounds on the likes of Sprout and many more.

Opener Kalibri is an exquisitely ethnic eight minute trip with warm, groovy drums and plenty of exquisite synths that light up the airwaves up top. It is hypnotic and heady and truly escapist, and features Ilan Oz (Ozart) on the piano, and Vitali on the violin for a classic touch. Umza Pumza is a darker tune with frazzled chords and tense percussion that keeps you on the edge of your seat and makes for a thrilling ride, super dramatic with psychedelic elements.

This is another majestic outing for this fast rising pair.
