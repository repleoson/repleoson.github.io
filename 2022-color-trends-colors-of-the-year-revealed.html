Time to talk color trends for 2022! In this video I go over the color palettes for 2022 from Benjamin Moore, Behr, Sherwin Williams and PPG including their colors of the year! Here’s a hint, everyone is seeing GREEN this year! All the big paint brands are predicting green is going to be the hot, trendy color for 2022. However there are some differences between how green is used in interior designs from each of these companies… so stay tuned as I break down all the similarities and differences in these very interesting palettes!
