Our eyes are exposed to harmful blue light every day from the frequent use of electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, personal computers etc., making them increasingly vulnerable. As a result, it can lead to the deterioration of our eye health. Hence, it is important to consume daily eye supplements that contain eye-protective nutrients like lutein, zeaxanthin, bilberry, vitamin C, vitamin E, zinc and selenium.
