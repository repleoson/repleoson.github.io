Our eyes are exposed to harmful blue light every day from the frequent use of electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, personal computers etc., making them increasingly vulnerable. Hence, it is important to consume daily eye supplements that contain eye-protective nutrients like lutein, zeaxanthin, bilberry, vitamin C, vitamin E, zinc and selenium.
