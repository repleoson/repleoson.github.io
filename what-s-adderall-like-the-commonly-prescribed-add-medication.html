Crystal meth doesn’t even have as bad of a comedown as adderall. Adderall XR is an extended release version of the drug which causes it to last longer. People need to study the adderall drug and how similar it is to the speed drug to understand the dangers. Prescription drugs aren’t always safe, don’t just trust your doctor.
