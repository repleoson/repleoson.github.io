You’re watching the official music video for Bette Midler’s international hit "From A Distance" from the 1990 album "Some People’s Lives". The song was written in 1987 by American singer-songwriter Julie Gold and first recorded by Nanci Griffith in the same year. "From a Distance" went to #1 on the Adult Contemporary chart and peaked at #2 on the Billboard Hot 100.
