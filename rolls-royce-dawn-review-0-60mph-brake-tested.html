This is the absolutely luxurious Rolls-Royce Dawn… And Mat’s on hand to see if it’s worth the absolutely huge £282,000 starting price!

Well for starters, when it comes to the quality of the interior, there’s no denying that Rolls-Royce is pretty much the best in the business. Every element of the interior is made from high-quality materials, and the touch of pretty much every button just exudes quality.

What’s more, the high-quality look & feel continues through to the exterior. However, unlike the regular Dawn, this is the Black Badge edition, so you’ll find a black grille & spirit of ecstasy at the front through to black exhaust surrounds at the rear, to name just a few design upgrades.

What’s more, the Black Badge edition doesn’t just change the styling - it means you’re treated to even more performance from the engine! So under the bonnet, you’ll find a 6.6-litre twin-turbo V12 that delivers 600hp & 840Nm of torque!

So… Is it worth it? There’s only one way to find out - join Mat for his latest in-depth review!
