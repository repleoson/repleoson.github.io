Most people have the ability to quit rather than the ability to succeed.

Quitability. Does that word exist? I don’t think it’s in the dictionary, but it’s a word that I’m very used to, and that is the ability to quit. Have You Got The Why Factor, is a book written by successful author and entrepreneur Ayo Olaseinde. This book is Ayo’s personal story of learning from both successes and failures and using them to achieve considerable success.

Through the life lessons of Ayo, you will learn how to achieve a healthy, successful and positive lifestyle. In order to become successful within the Why Factor, you need to remain open-minded and willing to learn.
The Why Factor derives from a characteristic within us that has been around since the beginning of mankind; asking the “why?” question.

By re-introducing the question “why?” in a positive manner into our daily lives, we can develop a better understanding of life and learn to build a happier, positive and more successful way of life. The Why Factor Academy, developed by Ayo Olaseinde, will empower you and teach you the fundamental skills to help you on your own journey to be the best you that you can be.
