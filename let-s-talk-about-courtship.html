Courtship or Dating? Which one should Christians do?

Many years ago a book called, "I kissed dating goodbye" came out by Joshua Harris. While there was TONS of very helpful and good information in that book many people seemed to swing to the other extreme and ended up heartbroken and bitter. So in this video, Emily and I talk about a few principles for healthier relationships whether you prefer to call it "courtship" or "Christian dating." Either way, we hope these tips will lead to better relationships!
