Courtship or Dating? Which one should Christians do?

Many years ago a book called, "I kissed dating goodbye" came out by Joshua Harris. So in this video, Emily and I talk about a few principles for healthier relationships whether you prefer to call it "courtship" or "Christian dating." Either way, we hope these tips will lead to better relationships!
