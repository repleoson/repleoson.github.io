As we enter into a new section in the book of Ephesians, Nathan Johnson explores Paul’s prayer in Ephesians 3:14-21 and the abundant life made available to us in Christ Jesus.

The focus of this lesson is Paul’s statement “for this reason” as he begins his prayer—building upon what He has written so far in Ephesians.

Join Nathan in this study where we review Ephesians 1-3 and what reason Paul is praying on behalf of believers.
