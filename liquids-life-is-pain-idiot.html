Liquids - Life Is Pain Idiot [2021]
Nail Biter Records

Now the definitive collection of liq tracks, life is pain idiot.

For as much as i love the old snotty production, it’s hard to argue that Liquids have ever sounded better than right here. I mean this shit sounds ridiculously good to me. By the time "more thana a friend" hit my ear, it triggered some weird nostalgia trip from when i used to listen to those not so old liquids 7’s on repeat (damn, it hasn’t felt like 5 years), and to me this record celebrates that feeling in the best way.

If you’re reading this Mat, thanks for all the tunes.

Most importantly, this record would not have been possible without Erik Nervous on the tambourine. He’s also responsible for recording the record. A+

There will be a vinyl release in the summer sometime. That’s soon to be announced I gather. It’ll feature a "scary artwork", but for what it’s worth I actually like this artwork. Leave it. Probably won’t though.
