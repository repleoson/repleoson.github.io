Over twenty participants from Momase region graduated with certificates after completing two weeks of SME Trainer of Trainers workshop in Lae.

The workshop was organized by the SMEC Corporation a government statutory authority under the department of Trade and Industry.

SMEC is working with all Provincial, District and LLG officers to reach the government’s goal of training over 6 million SME trainers by 2026.
