Happy Valentines Day!
“Constant” is a new song written by me and Jesse Barrera, produced by Jesse Barrera for his on-going project this year.

I’ve worked with Jesse for many years on a few of my projects including For More Than A Feeling LP, Pixelated EP, Suddenly, and Everything To Lose on my sophomore album, Pause (II).

I hope when you hear this song, it brings you feelings of love, happiness, and remind you of the people who have remained “constant” in your life.
