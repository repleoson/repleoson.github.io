About the artist: "There are other worlds within it that we have never, and may never experience. Worlds can be many shapes, sizes, and colors. Through various mediums, I attempt to interpret the worlds I dream, the worlds I believe exist, the worlds I think I have only experienced in my head." – Bobby English
