Watch this tutorial to learn how to make a concept map. Both expert and beginner diagrammers will learn how concept mapping works and how to make one. We will build a concept map from scratch and explain every step along the way.

Concept maps are a visual way to organize your thoughts and make connections between ideas. They improve our ability to understand and remember concepts, because our brains process visuals faster than plain text.

Concept maps have three elements: shapes, arrows, and text. The subject is at the top and the related ideas become more specific as you move down the map. In this way, concept maps are different from mind maps that just have information in every direction around a subject.

Concept maps are an excellent way to organize your thoughts and ideas!
