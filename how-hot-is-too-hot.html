Keeping CHTs under good control is one of the most important contributors to piston aircraft engine longevity. But how hot is too hot? Is cooler always better? And if CHTs are too hot, how can you figure out why and what can you do to correct it? In this webinar, Mike Busch A&P/IA offers the operational guidelines and troubleshooting techniques you need to keep your CHTs well-behaved.
