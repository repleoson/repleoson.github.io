"No Guarantee" is the fourth official single from Protoje’s (@Protoje) forthcoming fourth full length album "A Matter Of Time" to be released June 29, 2018.

Chronixx (@Chronixxmusic) joins Protoje on the track, pairing Protoje’s potent lyricism with a catchy yet poignant hook. Together they explore the human condition as it relates to topics of love and hate, envy, jealousy and loyalty.

“No Guarantee” available now, as an instant track with the pre-order of the album A Matter Of Time.
