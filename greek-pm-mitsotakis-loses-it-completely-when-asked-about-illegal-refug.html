"There has been overwhelming evidence and you keep denying and lying. This is like narcissistic abuse. Why are you not honest? Why don’t you say ‘Brussels left us alone, we waited for 6 years, nobody did anything. Now I have a say and yes, I do cruel, barbarian pushbacks", Ingeborg Beugel said and Mitsotakis, being used το flattering questions by greek journalists, lost it completely.
