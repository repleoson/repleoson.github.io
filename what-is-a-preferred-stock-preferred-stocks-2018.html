So, what is a preferred stock?
Imagine stocks and bonds got together and had a baby. That baby would be a preferred stock. Preferred stocks have features of both stocks and bonds. They are similar to bonds because they generally pay a fixed rate. For example. Let‚Äôs pretend a company issues a preferred stock.
