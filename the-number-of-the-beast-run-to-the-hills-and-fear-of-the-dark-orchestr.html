After endless sleepless nights, after countless hours of editing, I finally finished a new symphonic video dedicated to Iron Maiden. It is a new version of a previously published symphonic medley that includes the songs Fear of The Dark, The Number of The Beast, and Run to The Hills. It was recorded from a concert we gave just before the closure of activities and like the old times, the orchestra is massive and epic. The video and the music are edited in a way that you will feel like you are watching everything from your seat at the theater.


Line-Up:
Conductor: Javier Fernandez Prada.
Orchestra: "Orquesta Sinfónica de Chancay".
Timpani: Rodrigo Nuñez Melgar.
Drums: Hans Menacho.
Bass: Fabrizzio Huertas.
Guitar: Micky Tejada.
Orchestral Arrangements by Micky Tejada.
Produced by Micky Tejada.

Recorded at the "Teatro Municipal de Lima", 2019.
