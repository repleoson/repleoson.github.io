The batteries they are working on are a lot more interesting. StoreDot, today, is a pioneer of extreme fast-charging battery technology for electric vehicles. Over the past few years, StoreDot has managed to revolutionize the conventional Li-ion battery by designing and synthesizing proprietary organic and inorganic compounds.
