Official HD music video for “Sometimes” by Erasure.

The breakthrough single from Erasure (Vince Clarke & Andy Bell) was released in October 1986. The first release from the band’s second album,‘The Circus’, the single reached number 2 in the UK charts.

The iconic rooftop video was directed by Gerard De Thame, who also produced videos for Lloyd Cole, Black, Tinita Tikaram and The Damned.
