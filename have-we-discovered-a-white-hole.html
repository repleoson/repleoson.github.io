The endless expanses of the universe are characterized by the most diverse objects. In addition to the countless planets, moons and stars, it is above all black holes that cast their spell on us again and again. However, researchers suspect that black holes are not the only objects in space that leave us agape regarding their dimensions and galactic properties. What is currently only supported by mathematical calculations could possibly be proven beyond doubt one day: the existence of white holes!
