Hey guys, in this episode i"ll try to share with you everything I know about developing your illustration style!

So in this episode, I’ll talk about: why is actually important to develop a unique illustration style? 
Things you can do to improve your illustration work, and have a more specific and special illustration style.
And lastly, I’ll share my own personal story and show you how my illustration style has developed in the past years.
