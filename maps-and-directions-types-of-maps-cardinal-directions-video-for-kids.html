A map is an illustration of an area such as a city, a country, or a continent,
showing its main features. It is a drawing that gives special clue about an area.

The history of mapping can be traced to approximately 5,000 years ago.

Types of Maps:
General Reference map
Thematic map
Navigation Charts or Nautical Charts
Topographic map
Cadastral Maps
