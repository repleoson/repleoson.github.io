MENTAL HEALTH PRACTITIONER JOB INTERVIEW QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
Q1. Tell me about yourself and why you want to work in mental health?
Q2. Why do you want to work for our organisation as a Mental Health Practitioner?
Q3. What are the most important values in mental health and which, in your opinion, is the most important?
Q4. What skills and qualities are needed to work in mental health?
Q5. What does confidentiality mean to you?
Q6. What experience do you have within the Mental Health sector?
Q7. Tell me a time when you worked with other people within a healthcare team to provide excellent patient or client care?
Q8. What is safeguarding?
Q9. Explain a situation you were in when you helped to improve people’s lives?
Q10. Why should we choose you over the other candidates?
Q11. What challenges are we facing right now within the mental health sector?
Q12. Tell me a time when you had to change your style of communication to get your message across?
Q13. Talk me through the steps you would follow when dealing an upset or confused patient or client who was in your care?
Q14. What are your main strengths?
Q15. What’s your biggest weakness?
Q16. What would you do if you didn’t get on with someone in the mental health team?
Q17. How would you implement Health and Safety procedures as a Mental Health Practitioner?
Q18. Describe a stressful situation at work and how you handled it?
Q19. What is Clinical Governance?
Q20. How would you handle the stress of being a Mental Health Practitioner?
Q21. That’s the end of your Mental Health Practitioner interview. Do you have any questions?
