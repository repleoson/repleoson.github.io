Let’s take an in-depth look at some useful quantifiers that you will use in everyday conversation. These words are used to talk about an amount or quantity, but we use them in different ways and they are not always interchangeable.

In this lesson, I’ll share with you a lot of information so you can easily understand how and when to use these quantifiers. I’ll also talk about some of the slight nuances that differentiate these words from one another. And finally, I’ll give you my opinion as to how I personally use these quantifiers.
