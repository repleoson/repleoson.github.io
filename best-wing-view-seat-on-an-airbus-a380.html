I took one of BA’s A380 ‘Pilot Refresher’ flights across to Frankfurt for a Member Show in November as they were preparing their crews for the return to Long-haul service. I’ve taken the Liberty of giving her a title of ‘Luxury Liner’ and yes, I know the AA DC10’s were first with the name, but apart from the new comfy Big twins, I think this Big Mega Jet deserves the title. Sitting in this thing, even the Economy seats, is like getting a ride in a Rolls Royce: big wide comfy seats and comfy arm rests.
We were all on the lower deck, very few pax onboard, which was a delight and really did testament to the quietness of the cabin.
If you like to look out the window and want to see land below as well as a great shot of the Wing and the flight surfaces at work, then I suggest seat ‘A’ row 36, 37, 38 or 39. Or the other side depending on your preference, or neck strength - I do a lot of looking 😄
Anyhoo, enjoy this short flight from London Heathrow to Frankfurt. Check out the 600ft visibility landing at FRA!!!
