It’s been described as a ‘killer car fleet’.

A number of stolen luxury vehicles are being used by Sydney gangs to facilitate murder.

The cars were stolen from Sydney’s northern suburbs, and police believe the thieves are being paid big money to supply the vehicles.

Over a 9 day period, four homes were targeted. Five cars went missing.

Police have seized more than 40 stolen luxury cars, including the ones used in the attempted murder of Ibrahim Hamzy in North Sydney, as well as the double killing of Salim Hamze and Toufik Hamze at Guildford last month.
