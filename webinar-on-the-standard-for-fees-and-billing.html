The College of Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioners and Acupuncturists of Ontario (CTCMPAO) presents a webinar on the Standard of Practice for Fees and Billing. This webinar will help educate TCM Practitioners and Acupuncturists on what they need to know about the standard and fees and billing practices.

For more information, please see CTCMPAO’s Standard for Fees and Billing:
