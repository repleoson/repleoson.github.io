In this video we will see what is Block Inheritance and Enforced options in Group Policy in Windows Server 2019 Active Directory.

Blocking inheritance prevents the settings in GPOs that are linked to higher-level sites, domains, or organizational units from being automatically inherited by the specified domain or OU. On other end enforced option prevents a GPO from being overridden by other GPO.
