About Great Place to Work®:
Great Place to Work® is an international research and consulting company. We support organizations in more than 50 countries across the globe in developing an attractive, trust-based workplace and company culture which at the same time improves satisfaction of employees. Each year more than 10,000 companies worldwide take a Great Place to Work® employee survey.
