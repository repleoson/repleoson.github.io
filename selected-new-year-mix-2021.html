It’s time again for our biggest mix of the year. 🎉 Except that this year turned out in a way we could have ever expected. Even though it wasn’t a great year, it was full of insanely good music. In this New Year Mix we included some of our favorites in 2020 and two premieres of upcoming Selected releases earlier next year. Enjoy!
