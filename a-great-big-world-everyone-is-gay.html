Awhile ago, our friends Kristin and Dannielle (who run a website called EveryoneIsGay.com that offers advice to LGBTQ youth) challenged us to write the "gayest song ever" for a compilation. NO ONE deserves to be bullied or treated unfairly based on sexual preference. Diversity should be celebrated!

Thank you to Kristin and Dannielle at http://www.EveryoneIsGay.com for inspiring this!!!
