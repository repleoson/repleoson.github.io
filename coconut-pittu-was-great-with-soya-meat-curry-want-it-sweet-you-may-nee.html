When the steam sprays out of the top of the mould, it is an indication that the Pittu is fully streamed. Yet now some people cook hot spicy meat or TVP curry to go with Pittu as well. We truly love and enjoy eating Pittu with sugar, treacle and bananas ever since we were kids. Of course with coconut milk as it not only gives the Pittu a nutty coconut flavour but it makes each mouthful, easy to swallow.
