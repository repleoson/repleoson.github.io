How did global gaming company Electronic Arts go from being named "Worst Company in America" to clearing a billion dollars in profit? The most successful companies, from Amazon to Best Buy, understand their best customers are their most valuable asset, and they tailor their acquisition, development, and retention efforts to those customers.

In The Customer Centricity Playbook, Wharton School professor Peter Fader and Wharton Interactive‚Äôs executive director Sarah Toms help you see your customers as individuals rather than a monolith, so you can stop wasting resources by chasing down product sales to each and every consumer.

- Develop a customer-centric strategy for your organization
