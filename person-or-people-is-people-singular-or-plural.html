In this video, learn how to use "person" and "people" and learn if "people" is conjugated in 3rd person singular or 3rd person plural.

"Person" and "People" are two of the most common words in English. They are both nouns and frequently used as subjects.

Correct English grammar requires that you conjugate the verb with the subject.

But how do you conjugate "person" and "people".

Do you conjugate them as singular or plural?
