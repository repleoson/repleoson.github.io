Most canines can really benefit from being given tasks, jobs, and tricks to do. It helps keep them from becoming bored and usually for them it’s really fun and exciting.

Boredom can cause anxiety and destructive behaviors.

When we get foxes in that are around 6 months or younger they are at the perfect age for trick training and leash training. I at least attempt this with the foxes. Not all foxes are into being walked or learning tricks and if that’s the case I don’t force them. Then we need to come up with other ways to keep them busy.
