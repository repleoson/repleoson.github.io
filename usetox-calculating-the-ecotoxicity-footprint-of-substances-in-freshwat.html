A free software for calculating the ecotoxicity footprint of substances in freshwater

• Calculate the characterization factors matching your needs
• Carry out sensitivity analysis with your parameter values
• Get access to the whole documentation & data sources
• The “USEtox® model” in an easy to use software

Do you want to know the ecotoxicological impact of your products and provide this information to your customers?
With more than 7000 substances included, ecotoxicity footprint is an easy-to-use and comprehensive online software that allows to calculate your own ecotoxicological characterization factors using the USEtox® model.
