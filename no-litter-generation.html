There’s hardly any part of the world that isn’t affected by litter – on the ground and in lakes and seas. If we don’t do anything about it, our oceans could end up containing more plastic than fish by the year 2050! But you and other children and young people around the world can make a difference and become the No Litter Generation.

No Litter Generation is an an initiative organized by the World’s Children’s Prize (WCP) and Håll Sverige Rent (Keep Sweden Tidy). with support from the Swedish Postcode Lottery.

Learn more at http://worldschildrensprize.org/nolittergeneration
