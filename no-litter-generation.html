There’s hardly any part of the world that isn’t affected by litter – on the ground and in lakes and seas. But you and other children and young people around the world can make a difference and become the No Litter Generation.

No Litter Generation is an an initiative organized by the World’s Children’s Prize (WCP) and Håll Sverige Rent (Keep Sweden Tidy). with support from the Swedish Postcode Lottery.
