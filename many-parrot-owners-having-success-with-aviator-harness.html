This is a video of Parrot Wizard webinar students of the German Flight Club demonstrating how eagerly their parrots put on an Aviator Harness! Watch how well a Blue and Gold Macaw, Green Winged Macaw, African Grey, Amazon, and Senegal Parrot put on their Aviator Harnesses. This video was recorded at the 2018 annual convention of the German Aviator Club near Frankfurt.
