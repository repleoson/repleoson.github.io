Hello, hello! Welcome back to my channel. Hope everyone is well! I have been wanting to try something different with my videos. So here it is, a very refreshing Strawberry flavored Mojito in a new style!
My family & I absolutely loved it, hope you guys like it too.

Ingredients: (for 2 glasses of Mojito)

1 lemon
3tbs mint leaves
6-8 strawberries
1/2tsp black salt
1/4tsp black pepper powder
ice
sprite/7up

Method:

Take two glasses you want to serve the drink in. Add slices of lemon, mint leaves, strawberries, 1/2tsp black salt & 1/4tsp black pepper. Crush them followed by adding ice. Pour sprite & mix.
Your drink is ready to be served. Enjoy the refreshing taste!
