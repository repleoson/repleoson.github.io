ABOUT ISAIAH HOUSE TREATMENT CENTER
ISAIAH HOUSE TREATMENT CENTER is a residential, intensive outpatient and outpatient treatment program with a Gold Standard of care for men & women. We are nationally accredited by Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities (CARF), and dual-licensed with the state of Kentucky as an Alcohol and Other Drug Entity (AODE).

ISAIAH HOUSE TREATMENT CENTER provides short Medicaid (28 days) treatment and long (8-11 month) treatment with 27 sober living homes for transitional living. They have a total of 156 beds at Patricia’s Place and Isaiah House on our Willisburg, KY campus as well as The Jake Kuertz & Greg Bowen Center, in Chaplin, KY, and the new Lisa Walker Center for women in Harrodsburg, KY. They also offer Outpatient treatment in Danville, KY. Medication Assisted Treatment (MAT) is available through Isaiah House programs.

For more information, please call 859-375-9200 or visit www.isaiah-house.org
