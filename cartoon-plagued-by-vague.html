Plagued by Vague is the first track from Cartoon’s EP The Annexe, under the TWA Records Label. Manufactured & distributed throughout Australia in 1996 under licence from Rocking Horse Recordings.

Cartoon were a three piece hailing from Lismore, NSW with a fat, funky vibe ranging from smooth to frantic in its delivery.

The lineup consisted of Pawl Andersen on Bass & some Vocals, Pall Archer on Guitars and most Vocals and Dug Threlfall on Drums and other Vocals.
