http://drinkspottr.com – Drinkspottr joins forces with Bartender Benjamin Wood of Distilled NY to show how to make the classic Old Fashioned Cocktail.

Make sure to like, comment and subscribe to this channel for more Untapped, brought to you by Drinkspottr, the lifestyle guide for the sophisticated drinker.

Ingredients:
1 Sugar Cube
2 Dashes of Angostura Bitters
1 Dash Regan’s Orange Bitters
1 Bar Spoon of Soda Water
2 Ounces Bourbon or Rye
Ice: 5 KD Cubes or Equivalent
Glass: Double, Old-Fashioned
Garnish: Orange Oil/Orange Twist, Lemon Oil/Lemon Twist
