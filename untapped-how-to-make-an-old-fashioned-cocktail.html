Ingredients:
1 Sugar Cube
2 Dashes of Angostura Bitters
1 Dash Regan’s Orange Bitters
1 Bar Spoon of Soda Water
2 Ounces Bourbon or Rye
Ice: 5 KD Cubes or Equivalent
Glass: Double, Old-Fashioned
Garnish: Orange Oil/Orange Twist, Lemon Oil/Lemon Twist
