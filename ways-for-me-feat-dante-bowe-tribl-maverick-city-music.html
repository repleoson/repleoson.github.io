Official Music Video for "Ways For Me" featuring Dante Bowe.

“Ways For Me” was written by Tywan Mack, Dante Bowe & Aaron Moses. It is from Tribl Records’ newest album, ‘Tribl Nights Atlanta’ Available Now!

Tribl is where artists with different stories come to share and experience the genesis of a new worship sound. To worship together with raw, unique, powerful and authentic praise.
