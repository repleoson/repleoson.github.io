A Bargain Lover’s Paradise

The Outlet Village is a luxury discount shopping mall in Jebel Ali, Dubai.
It is modeled on the village of San Gimignano, a picturesque medieval hill town in Tuscany, Italy.The design incorporates stone walls, piazzas, tiled flooring and water fountains. It is set on one level and completely enclosed with a glass roof.

Brands like Adidas, Calvin Klein, Armani, DKNY, Roberto Cavalli and Tommy Hilfiger are just some of the brands among the selection of 100+ stores.
