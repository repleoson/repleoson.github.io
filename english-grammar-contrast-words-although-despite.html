In this video you will learn how to use the subordinating conjunctions ‘although’, ‘though’, ‘despite the fact that’, and ‘even though’, and the conjunctions ‘despite’ and ‘in spite of’ to show a contrast of surprising facts.

You will also learn how to use gerund clauses.

At the end of the video you will find three exercises to help you practice what you learned.
