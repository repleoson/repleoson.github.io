Welcome to Hey Bear Sensory!

This video is not a traditional counting tool, and you won’t see any numbers. But we encourage you to use the the scenes within to encourage and introduce counting, for example, there’s five blueberries and they all jump five times. Maybe get some blueberries out of the fridge and count with them as well ( and eat them!). The thought process behind this was that most ‘learning’ or counting tools are focused on remembering the numerical symbol, for example seeing numbers lined up 1-10. You can count the movements, the characters or both. Theres plenty of opportunity to learn, or just enjoy your favourite characters!
