Eyewitness News took a trip to the Thaba Chweu municipality in Mpumalanga to assess the state of what was once a beautiful tourist attraction, the small town of Mashishing, formerly known as Lydenburg. The dorpie once received numerous awards lauding its cleanliness, and the municipality for its efficiency. But residents say the town is now a shadow of its former self.
