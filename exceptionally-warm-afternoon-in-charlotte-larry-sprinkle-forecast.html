Sunny and warm with highs in the low to mid-80s. Afternoon temperatures will be 10-15 degrees above average. The record high goes back to 1954: 91 degrees.



Thursday and Friday:



Sunny and unseasonably warm again with highs in the in the mid 80s Thursday and mid to upper 80s Friday. The record high those two days are 87 in 1940 and 88 inn 1985.



The Weekend:
