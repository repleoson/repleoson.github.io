WHY IS THE INTERVIEWER ASKING YOU THE COMMON INTERVIEW QUESTION QUESTION, WHAT ARE YOU PASSIONATE ABOUT?
Well, if you are passionate about things in your life, it tells the hiring manager you are someone who is capable of getting deeply involved in something you enjoy doing. It also tells them you can be COMMITTED to one thing, and see it through to the end!

3 TIPS FOR ANSWERING THE INTERVIEW QUESTION, WHAT ARE YOU PASSIONATE ABOUT?
TIP #1. Give more than one thing that you are passionate about in your life. You could be passionate about lots of things – it could be work-related, a sport or physical activity, a pastime, or even a worthy cause or charity.

TIP #2. And, this is key when answering the interview question, what are you passionate about? Include in your answer a positive OUTCOME from your passion.

So, you could say… “I am very passionate about playing the guitar. I taught myself how to play by following online YouTube tutorials, and my goal was to always be able to play in a band. I actually achieved that goal last month when I did my first gig. It was a great feeling to be able to go from never having picked up a guitar before to doing a live gig in a relatively short space of time.”

That answer tells the hiring manager you set yourself goals, you apply yourself, and you follow things through until the end… i.e. there is a positive OUTCOME from your passion!

TIP #3. Is a very simple tip, but still very important… and that is to talk with PASSION and ENTHUSIASM when you are giving your answer!
