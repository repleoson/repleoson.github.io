On a Chanukah night in winter of 2015, Levy Falkowitz sang this classic song "Crack of Dawn" composed by legendary Yossi Green, written and originally recorded by MBD in the year 1992 on the hit album Moshiach.

Joining him is the very talented Pinny Ostreicher on the piano and the famous guitar player from the Sympinny band Chaim Bokchin.

Backup vocals by Avrum Mordche Schwartz and Eli Shprei from The Shira Choir.
