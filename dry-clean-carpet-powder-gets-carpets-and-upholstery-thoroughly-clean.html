Get the same results as professional dry cleaners, at a fraction of the price. This powerful dry cleaning powder lets you clean fresh and old stains on rugs, carpets and upholstery in your home, office, even the car. Suitable for pile up to 2cm, simply sprinkle, brush in and hoover up the residue, with the stains disappearing almost instantly. Your carpets are left clean, with a natural fresh scent and dry, so can be walked on immediately. Supplied in 3x440g bags, each bag covers 75 - 130 sq.ft.
