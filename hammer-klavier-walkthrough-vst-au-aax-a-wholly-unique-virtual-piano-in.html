Impact Soundworks developer Andrew Aversa showcases HAMMER KLAVIER, an avant-garde instrument sampled from an upright piano that has had its hammers replaced with real metal hammers. Available now for Kontakt Player (VST, AU, AAX), and created in partnership with musical mad scientist@Mattias Krantz, HAMMER KLAVIER offers a new sound, the dynamic responsiveness of a regular piano, and much greater versatility than the typical prepared piano!
