THE TEX-MEX

1. CHAPULTEPEC LUPITA
ADDRESS: 813 Richmond Ave, Houston, TX 77006
OPERATION HOURS: Everyday 7AM - 4AM
INTERVIEWEE: Maria Guadalupe Gonozalez & Alicia Gonzalez

🌮 BREAKFAST TACOS: Add eggs and scramble together. Add bacon and potatoes and continue chopping on the stovetop. Take flour tortillas and warm them up in the oven. ﻿Place the scrambled egg mixture in the tortilla and top with white and yellow cheese mixture and pico de gallo. ﻿Roll it up and enjoy.

🌯CHIMICHANGA: On the flat top grill, add fajita beef and allow it to grill. Take a large tortilla and add fajita beef. Add beans on top of beef. Add white and yellow cheese mixture. Fold it up, securing it with toothpicks. Place in the fryer under the frying basket just to make it crispy.

💸PRICE: $12.95

2. MOLINA’S CANTINA
ADDRESS: 7901 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77063, United States
OPERATION HOURS: Sun-Thurs 11AM-9PM		 Fri-Sat 11AM-10PM
WEBSITE: https://www.molinascantina.com/
INTERVIEWEE: ﻿Ricardo Molina

🧀 THE ORIGINAL JOSE’S DIP: Chili con queso blended with spicy taco meat.

💸 PRICE: $8.95 (Large)

🍳 ENCHILADAS DE TEJAS: Take a corn tortilla and dip it in a chile ancho/water blend. Fry in oil at low/medium heat (you want to soften the tortilla, not fry it). Pat tortilla with paper towel to remove excess oil. Roll with desired filling inside (mix of American and cheddar cheese). Place in baking dish and cover with chile con carne, shredded cheese, add diced onions. Place in oven at 350 for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted throughout. If you’re feeling adventurous, top with a fried egg.

💸 PRICE: $13.45 (with fried egg)

3. EL TIEMPO CANTINA
ADDRESS: 2814 Navigation Blvd, Houston, TX 77003, United States
OPERATION HOURS: Mon-Thurs 11AM-9PM		 Fri 11AM-10PM Sat 9AM-10PM Sun 9AM-9PM
WEBSITE: https://www.eltiempocantina.com/
INTERVIEWEE: Domenic Laurenzo

🥩 BEEF FAJITAS: Place skirt steak on grill. Add secret marinade called Agra negra to give it the color and flavor. Add seasoning (mixed spices), chopped peppers and onion and continue cooking. ﻿Place in custom made skillet. ﻿Serve with warm tortillas.

💸PRICE: $1/4 lb 22.99		 Half Pound 38.99 Full Pound 53.99
