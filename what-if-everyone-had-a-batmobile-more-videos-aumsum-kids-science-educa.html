Fifthly, if Everyone had a Batmobile, Batmobiles are expensive to maintain, heaps of abandoned Batmobiles may be visible everywhere.
Lastly, if Everyone had a Batmobile, some people may get used to the fancy toys of a Batmobile, adjusting back to an old car might prove difficult.
