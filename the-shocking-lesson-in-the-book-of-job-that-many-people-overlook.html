Why do the righteous suffer? This is the question raised after Job loses his family, his wealth, and his health. Job questions God Himself and learns valuable lessons about the sovereignty of God and his need to totally trust in the Lord. Job is then restored to health, happiness, and prosperity beyond his earlier state. Faith Triumphant Over Experience!
