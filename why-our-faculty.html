Welcome to the fifth video of our English study series.
In this one we would like to tell you more about our student city, student life, culture, food, free time activities, possibilities after graduation, study materials and so much more.

When searching for university studies finding the right place, you will like and enjoy is very important as it will be your new home for several years and you will form very important relationships and connections, professional and personal too.
