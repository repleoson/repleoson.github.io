The Queen has criticised world leaders’ inaction on addressing the climate crisis, saying she was ‘irritated’ by individuals who ‘talk but don’t do’.

She made the remarks, which were picked up on a livestream, at the opening of the Welsh parliament in Cardiff on Thursday during a conversation with the Duchess of Cornwall and Elin Jones, the parliament’s presiding officer.
