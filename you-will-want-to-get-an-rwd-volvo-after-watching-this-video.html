After traveling all around Scandinavia and visiting various automotive events, we could clearly see that the locals really love their own Nordic car brands, especially one brand in particular - Volvo. And there is no wonder why. Almost all Volvo’s up until early 90’s were rear wheel drive, robust as hell and pretty darn good looking if you are into "sharp" and "boxy" cars. It’s a perfect combination for anyone who wants to build a fun drift car. We had a lot of nice shots with old turbobricks going sideways, parked as stance cars and just still being used as a daily car and that’s why we decided to throw some of the clips together and show them off. Enjoy the mixture of 740’s, 240’s, 940’s and some other bricks rolling/drifting around!
