Real Madrid are ready for their return to action after completing their final training session ahead of Tuesday night’s Champions League match against Shakhtar Donetsk. Karim Benzema, Toni Kroos, Thibaut Courtois and the rest of Carlo Ancelotti’s players trained at Kiev’s Olympic Stadium, which played host to the Champions League final in 2018 in which Real Madrid beat Liverpool 3-1.
