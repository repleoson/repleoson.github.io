Working with Lemons presents "You Will Be Found" from the Broadway musical DEAR EVAN HANSEN. #youwillbefound

A letter that was never meant to be seen, a lie that was never meant to be told, a life he never dreamed he could have. Evan Hansen is about to get the one thing he’s always wanted: a chance to finally fit in.

Both deeply personal and profoundly contemporary, DEAR EVAN HANSEN is a new American musical about life and the way we live it.

The original Broadway musical won both a Grammy and a Tony, particularly for Ben Platt’s fantastic performance. The musical and lyrics have helped start discussions and awareness of mental illness and youth suicide.
