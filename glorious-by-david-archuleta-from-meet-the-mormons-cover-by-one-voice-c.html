The Story:
This video was almost fully produced in 24 hours. On Friday, October 31, Masa Fukuda thought it would be fun for the One Voice Children‚Äôs Choir to record a cover of David Archuleta‚Äôs ‚ÄúGlorious‚Äù from the film Meet the Mormons, and submit it for competition. Between 3:00 and 7:00 p.m., Masa created this beautiful arrangement, developed practice tracks and produced sheet music for the students of the choir. At 1:00 p.m., 63 members of the choir began arriving to film while Masa finished mixing in his studio. At 1:30 p.m., he arrived with the final recording and we then taped the entire video and wrapped by 3:00 p.m. Sometimes the pieces just fall into place!
