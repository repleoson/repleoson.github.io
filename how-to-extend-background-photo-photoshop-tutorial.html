Learn how to extend the background on a photo in Photoshop. This Photoshop tutorial, I will show you 2 ways on how to stretch and increase the background. First technique is Content-Aware Scale and the second is using the Rectangular Marquee Tool with Content-Aware. This is perfect for photographers if they want a wide angle look to their photography.


After extending the size and width of the background, I will show you 3 tools to touch up your photo.
