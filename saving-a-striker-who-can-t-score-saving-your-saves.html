We all have an understanding of who should be scoring our goals in Football Manager. Even if we are doing well in our FM save if the goals are coming from areas we don’t expect our striker, not scoring can create a feeling of anxiety. Your striker needs to score goals for you to be successful long term. That is the prevailing wisdom of football manager. I suppose I’m here to help you figure out how to fix this problem but also teach you when to ignore it because it is not always true.

We spend a good deal working on finances in this save your saves. There are club planning parts and other things that guide you to football manager glory. I am trying to pick a collection of different tough saves to provide a broad range of experiences to make you a better FM player. It is also the last SYS on FM21, so I hope we covered enough bases to help you enjoy FM21 for years to come if you choose to not buy another FM for a few years.
