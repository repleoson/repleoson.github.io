This video focuses on Aristotle’s work, the Categories, and examines his listing in chapter 4 of the ten categories or predicables. These are main ways that uncombined terms signify something.

These are: substance (ousia), Quantity (poson), Quality (poion), Relation (pros ti), Place (pou), Time (pote), Position (keisthai), State (ekhein),(Action (poiein) and Passion (pashkein).
