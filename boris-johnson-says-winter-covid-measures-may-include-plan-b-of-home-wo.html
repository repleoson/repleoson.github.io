Outlining the government’s winter Covid-19 plan, Boris Johnson said the government will ‘will keep further measures in reserve. A plan B.’ These measures include face masks, advise to work from home and vaccine passports, the prime minister said.

Speaking at a No 10 press conference, Johnson added that ‘it might still make the difference between keeping businesses open at full capacity or not’.
