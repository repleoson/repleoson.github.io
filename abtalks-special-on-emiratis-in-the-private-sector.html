This Special address a topic that concerns most Emiratis. Adel Alfalasi, Fuad Mansoor Sharaf and Mouza Aldhaheri are all Emiratis who currently work within the private sector in the UAE. They talk about stability in the private sector, career growth, opportunities and education, as well as the hesitations and worries when it comes to not following the general expectation of working in the government sector.
