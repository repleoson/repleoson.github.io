Just because the car has been running fine, doesn’t mean the car is all fixed…

Today, I address one of this car’s biggest issues. The steering angle sensor. Apparently, the steering angle sensor (SAS) ties together several different modules on the Volvo V70R, and if it works… 3 other modules should work properly. I have my buddy Jacob in town to help me, and the folks at @XeMODeX Inc have been helping me throughout the entire process…. This should be great!
