We do not want to miss a high probability day trading opportunity just because of poor option liquidity. Options day traders favor weekly in the money options that have a delta greater than .65. When we are swing trading options we favor selling out of the money bullish put spreads so that we can take advantage of accelerated time decay and we can distance ourselves from the action.
