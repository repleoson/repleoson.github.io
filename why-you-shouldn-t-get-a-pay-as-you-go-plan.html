David and David explain why you shouldn’t sign up for a pay-as-you-go cell phone plan.

Firstly, pay-as-you-go is a bit of a misleading phrase. What you’re really doing is giving your carrier money upfront, and they’re deducting from that total as you go.

These plans are outdated and, a lot of the time, the amount you pay to the carrier is more than you’d pay if you signed up for a regular prepaid plan.
