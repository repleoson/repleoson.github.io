Daku suka dengan makanan turkish terutamanya "meal of the day" di restoran turkish. Nanti one day nak cuba buat masakan mereka insyaAllah.

Untuk hari ini, jom buat TURKISH BREAD tapi daku modify lah of course.

TURKISH MOZARELLA BREAD
__________
1kg tepung gandum / 1kg wheat flour
2sk garam / 2tsp salt
4sk yis / 4tsp instant yeast
300ml susu suam / 300ml warm milk
300ml air suam / 300ml warm water
6sk minyak zaitun / 6 tsp olive oil
(After kneading, cover and let it rise for 1hour)

Keju / mozarella
Mentega / butter

Glazing
..Mentega cair / melted butter
..daun parsli / chopped parsley
