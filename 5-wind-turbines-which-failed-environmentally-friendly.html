Fire has a huge financial impact on the industry; Each windmill costs more than ¬£2 million and generates an estimated income of more than ¬£500,000 per year. Any loss or downtime of these valuable assets makes the industry less viable and productive.

Wind turbines catch fire because highly flammable materials such as hydraulic oil and plastics are in close proximity to machinery and electrical wires. These can ignite a fire if they overheat or are faulty. Lots of oxygen, in the form of high winds, can quickly fan a fire inside a turbine.
