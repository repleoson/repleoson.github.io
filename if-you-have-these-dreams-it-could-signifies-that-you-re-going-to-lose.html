What is the meaning of a railway accident? Drinking Beer? Helping a victim or a dream about Adam and Eve? This video will explain it in a short way what these types of dream mean in your wakening life. 😟

• Dream about a railway accident signifies the loss of money

• To help a victim you may expect to be betrayed by a friend who will cheat you of some money you expected to receive.

• If you fall into an abyss "way down in the depths", then be careful in your business affairs. Do not lend money, for it will not be returned to you.

• When you cannot remove your foot from the speeding vehicle accelerator, be aware of gambling and drinking!

• If you dream of Adam and Eve, and Adam looks displeased and angry with you, use great caution in all your dealings: do not travel by sea, and do not borrow or lend money for at least two months.

• To dream of a property agent or agency coming to you or your home, signifies the loss of inheritance.

• If you dream of a fast moving airship, be wary of investing your money, as it foretells great loss.

• To dream of carrying an empty bag signifies approaching poverty.

• When you dream of an empty farm barn you need to be prepared for some calamity involving the loss of your money.

• I know that in the wakening life, drinking beer has a powerful and relaxing omen, but to dream that you are drinking beer, you may take it as a prediction, that unless you’re not careful, you will lose a large sum of money.

• Keep your money on you when you had a dream that you are betting on something. This dream warns you that you need to be careful of your money. Don’t lend money to anyone; otherwise you will lose it all.

• Birds usually signify lawsuits, love, joy or wedding. But if you see black birds, especially if they enter a house, this is bad, because it foretells approaching losses, sometimes of friends but most often of money.
