Welcome to the off-road race battle you didn’t know you needed in your life… Until today! Audi RSQ8 vs new Dacia Duster!

That’s right, we’re seeing if this awesome Audi performance SUV has what it takes to go toe-to-toe with the cheap & cheerful Dacia Duster through a series of off-road challenges.

When it comes to price, there’s a massive gulf between the two. The Duster we have here starts at around £20,000, whereas the RSQ8 comes in at a whopping £107,000!! But that’s not the only difference…

When you take a look under the RSQ8’s bonnet, you’ll find a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 that can deliver 600hp & 800Nm. It’s also equipped with the awesome Quattro all-wheel-drive system, and it’s mightily heavy, weighing around 2,300kg!

As for the Duster, it’s a whole lot lighter, tipping the scales at just 1,400kg! And as you’d expect, it’s well down on power, with a 1.5-litre turbo diesel engine putting down 115hp & 260Nm.

Surely it’ll be an easy win for the Duster, right?! There’s only one way to find out… LET’S DO THIS!
