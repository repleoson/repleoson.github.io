This video abstract by Utku Ali Riza Alpaydin presents a paper about the importance of geographical proximity for co-publication partnerships between universities and multinational enterprises. The paper entitled "Exploring the spatial reach of co-publication partnerships of multinational enterprises: to what extent does geographical proximity matter?" is published in Regional Studies, Regional Science, Volume 6, Issue 1, 2019. The paper is accessible at https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/21681376.2019.1583601
