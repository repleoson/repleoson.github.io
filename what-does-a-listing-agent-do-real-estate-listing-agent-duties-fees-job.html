They will add a specialized service to your agency while creating and handling more business leads to grow your bottom line.

What does a listing agent do? For starters, the real estate listing agent role tackles listing appointments. Real estate listing agent duties include preparing homes to list and close transactions. As the team grows, the real estate listing agent responsibilities can be broken down into team leader and listing specialists.
