I Am Dependable:

In this story, Spot let his friends and the community down when he didn’t show up to build Red Fox&#039;s den. Because he wasn’t there to help, his friends worked hard all day and couldn’t take time to eat or rest. After talking and cooperating with Bo, Spot gets creative and finds a way to make it up to his forest friends.

Cooperation
