Who the ** are "THE ADMINISTRATORS)

You may well ask.Surprisingly enough "The Administrators" were one of the Uk’s most successful Reggae Acts in the 80s and early 90s

Racking up a string of consecutive no1 hits and received numerous awards including "Best Reggae Band" But with all

these acolades, the band never managed to record an album,and for some even more obscure reason,fans of good music

outside the Uk were denied the opportunity to discover what we believe were one of the Uks finest exports…………….

Until now. POTENTIAL RECORDS and GROOVE AND A QUARTER RECORDS have combined to put together the definative

Administrators album entitled"AND THEN THERE WERE TWO"(THE ADMINISTRATORS

GREATEST HITS) Which features all "The Administrators " hits,previously unreleased tracks plus new compositions

from the new streamlined duo (hence the title) As well as two videos included on the album.We expect strong demand for this product."THE ADMINISTRATORS" will be playing a few special "one -of gigs" keep posted here or for up to the minute news /competitons/promos and giveawaways log on to POTENTIALRECORDS.COM
