The GV80 is the first ever SUV to be built by Genesis for 2021. Under the hood is a 3.5L twin-turbo V6 that is producing 375HP & mated to an 8-speed automatic. On the outside you will see a large grill, full LED lighting, 22in wheels, and 6-piston brakes. On the inside you will be greeted by full Nappa leather interior, 14in infotainment screen, and 3-D digital gauge display. Is the 2021 Genesis GV80 a BETTER sport SUV than a 2022 Acura MDX Type S?
