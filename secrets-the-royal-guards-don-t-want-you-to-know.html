It’s easy to think of the Queen’s Guard as some human statues who do not even blink as the world goes on around them. You’d think they’re robots built to stand all day by Buckingham palace and photo bomb those willing to have a snap for Instagram.

However, they are humans like us. They simply have undergone extensive training to carry out their duties as they do.

They’re responsible for defending the royal family, who, as everyone knows, is a fairly high-profile family infamous for keeping their private lives quiet. Very quiet, in fact.

Logically, anyone tasked with protecting a family with more than a few skeletons in their closet is certain to have a few secrets they don’t want you to know, right?

Well, in today’s video, we’ll help you peep behind the royal curtains and allow you in on some of the secrets of the royal guards the royal family hopes you never find out.
