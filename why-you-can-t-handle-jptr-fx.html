This week we talk with pedal-maker extraordinaire Chris Jupiter (founder and owner of JPTR FX) who explains that "music is about enjoying cool things, not the right things." We take a walk through the JPTR FX pedal line, including the Fernweh Delay, Warlow Fuzz and Jive Distortion, which are 100% as cool as they sound.
