Finally, girls with 330 to 400 points can proudly call themselves Type A. You are energetic and driven. You strive for perfection in everything you do. You only want the best in life and you get it because you work hard and believe in what you do. But, you know, it’s okay to sometimes give yourself a little break on your way to success.
