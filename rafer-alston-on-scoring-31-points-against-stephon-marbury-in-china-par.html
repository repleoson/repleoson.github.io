In the latest clip, Rafer Alston responded to reports that he faked an injury to get out of playing professional basketball in China. He claimed that he only wanted to return to the U.S. to bury his best friend, but Chinese culture says those types of excuses should only be made for immediate family. Alston claimed he’s returned to China multiple times since the fallout and has been embraced with open arms.
