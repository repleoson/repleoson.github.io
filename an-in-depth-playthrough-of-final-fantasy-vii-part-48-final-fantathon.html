Final Fantathon is our quest to casually Let’s Play every mainline FF game, but FFVII is a special case. So instead, we are doing an "In-depth Playthrough" of the game, where I talk about all of the interesting trivia, coding mechanisms, AI quirks, and lore material we as a community can think of as we go through the game.
