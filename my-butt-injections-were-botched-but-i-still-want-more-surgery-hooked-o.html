A SELF-confessed plastic surgery addict has spent over $100,000 sculpting her dream body. Staci Doll has had breast augmentation, lipo and fat transfer procedures as well as fillers and botox - and she is not done yet. Staci told Truly: “I would definitely consider myself a plastic surgery addict - who wouldn’t be addicted to ‘perfect’?” Staci’s plastic surgery transformation began when she was 18 years old with butt injections to give her more curves. However, Staci did not go to a legitimate practitioner and has since suffered horrendous pain, issues with them migrating up her back and consequent trips to the ER. While Staci loves her look overall now, she warns against people getting procedures done the same way. Staci runs an online boutique and she loves her ‘over the top’ lifestyle including designer shoes and accessories.Today, she will be revealing to her friends her imminent plastic surgery plans - but will they approve?
