Roy Davis is a retired travel agent living on a houseboat in Florida. He believes he is dismantling The Deep State as a self-proclaimed soldier in General Michael Flynn’s digital army. He’s also estranged from his family and looking for love. What causes people like Roy to become so resentful of our political system? We hang out with Roy and his friends to find out what draws them to cult-like beliefs.

In The Deep End, Vice News correspondent Alice Hines goes on a journey to the outer limits of obsession.
