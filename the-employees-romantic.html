Belgian "punk" band of the early 1980’s. Sharp, witty lyrics. Sharp witty graphics. Sharp, witty sound. Their range of styles varied from ska to punk to pop music. In Wit-Lof from Belgium, the song was described as being "ska at the speed of sound".
