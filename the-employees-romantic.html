Belgian "punk" band of the early 1980’s. Sharp, witty lyrics. Sharp witty graphics. Sharp, witty sound. Their range of styles varied from ska to punk to pop music. In Wit-Lof from Belgium, the song was described as being "ska at the speed of sound". The Offspring, a renound American punk band in the nineties, would later incorportate a lot of ideas from "Pick it up" into their song "Don’t Pick it up" on the cd "Ixnay on the Hombre", but it didn’t came to a lawsuit.
