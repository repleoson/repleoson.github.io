Common’s ‘A Beautiful Revolution, Part 2’ album teems with big ideas, great grooves, and fierce flows. Cultural pride and wishes for a better life for any of us who may be feeling downpressed in these chaotic times. “Imagine” is response to such pressure, a wish or a prayer that better days come soon. It was big treat to have the maestro hit our Brooklyn studios and spit this one. Heads up, greatness is in the house.
