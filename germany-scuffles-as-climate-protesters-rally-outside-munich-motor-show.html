Climate activists were seen scuffling with police outside of the International Motor Show (IAA) in Munich on Friday.
Hundreds of protesters from various climate and political organisations marched from their camp at Theresienwiese to nearby central Munich.
Police used force and pepper spray against the protesters some requiring medical attention.
This year’s edition of the IAA has experienced various protests and actions from climate groups over the past week.
