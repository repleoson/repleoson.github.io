BRILLIANT DIYs AND CRAFTS FOR EVERYTHING THAT YOU OWN



It’s time to let your creativity shine using these amazing tips and tricks you’ll love. In this video, I show you how decorate and come up with your own useful tools and designs.

Take some canvas fabric and lay it on the floor. Then, fill a small bucket with paint, step in it with one foot and then dance around on the paper to give it a very unique print-look.

You can give some color to your phone case using nail polish. Take various nail polish colors and pour them on your phone case. Then, using a hair drier let them dry and voila.

You can also use spray paint on a clear phone case to give your phone a galaxy look. You’ll need some newspaper for some texture and then some round stencils for the planets

To make your phone resemble a melted ice cream use some colored hot glue for the ice-cream and then come acrylic paint for the cone.

Watch until the end to see how you can give fun patterns to stuff around you using water and nail polish.
