I got the idea at a barbecue where there were also toothpicks lying around. I made a test picture and was thrilled. This painting method creates very beautiful works of art.

The acrylic paints I diluted with water before painting, which colors I use and how the correct mixing ratio is made you can see in this step by step video: https://youtu.be/cagHGb0oapo

With this technique you must have some patience, the acrylic paints must flow slowly over the edges of the toothpicks. This is the only way to get a nice mixture.

If you want to paint more pictures, you can reuse the toothpicks, you just have to let them dry.
