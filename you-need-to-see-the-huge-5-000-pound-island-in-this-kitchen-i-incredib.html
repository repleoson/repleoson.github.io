What do you do when you have a family of nine? If you’re Jocelyn Chiappone, you design a spacious, beautiful kitchen, that truly suits every imaginable need. Clean and classic, the kitchen’s beauty lies in the details. From the mosaic that acts as the backsplash behind the range ovens, to the walnut section of the island’s countertop, the space is full of hidden gems. Not as hidden is the island itself, which weighs around 2.5 tons, and is quite the workhorse, with a marble countertop, ample storage, and a deep sink to work with. But while the kitchen is certainly replete with practical amenities, it loses none of the potentials for coziness, as it is home to a small fireplace, and a welcoming dining area.
