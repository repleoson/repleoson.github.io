Herman Narula is CEO of Improbable, the London-based startup that enables developers to build huge, complex simulations using thousands of machines in the cloud via their SpatialOS platform. Want to know what happens to a city when all the cars switch to Autonomous Vehicles? Improbabl​e​ may be able to tell us.
