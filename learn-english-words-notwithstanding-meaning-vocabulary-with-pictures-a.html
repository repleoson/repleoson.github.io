What does Notwithstanding mean? Learn the meaning of Notwithstanding as we define this advanced vocabulary word with a simple definition, pictures, example sentences, English pronunciation and audio.

Notwithstanding definition (adverb) despite; regardless

Notwithstanding pronunciation: not-with-stan-ding

Examples of Notwithstanding in a sentence:

1. Notwithstanding his injured knee, the football player made two touchdowns.

2. I have to pick up my daughter from school, notwithstanding my headache.

3. Notwithstanding the fact he was grounded, Phil went to the cookout.

4. My wife really wanted to buy the antique car, notwithstanding my objections.

5. Notwithstanding her awful childhood, Marsha was a wonderful mother to her own children.
