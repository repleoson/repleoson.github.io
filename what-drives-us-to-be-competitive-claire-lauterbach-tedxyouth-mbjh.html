Competition: Does it make you better or bitter? Claire explores the intrinsic nature of competition and its impact on perceptions of ourselves and relationships with others.

Claire Lauterbach is an eighth-grade student at Mountain Brook Junior High and a member of the MBJH TEDEd Club. She loves to play piano, listen to music, and write. She is a member of the Mountain Brook debate team, and the Mountain Brook track team, meaning she can argue for long periods of time without getting winded.
