Fantastic DIY soap and candle ideas to surprise your friends and family members

Hey 5-minute crafters, let’s make some fabulous DIY soaps and candles to make your place cozier? Mix water and alginate powred to create a base using which you can make soap of any shape you want. Put your face, leg, hand, or anything in this mix and when it copies the shape, fill the hole with liquid soap. Or you can fill it with wax and create a unique candle!
