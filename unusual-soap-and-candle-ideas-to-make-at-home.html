Fantastic DIY soap and candle ideas to surprise your friends and family members

Hey 5-minute crafters, let’s make some fabulous DIY soaps and candles to make your place cozier? Mix water and alginate powred to create a base using which you can make soap of any shape you want. Put your face, leg, hand, or anything in this mix and when it copies the shape, fill the hole with liquid soap. Or you can fill it with wax and create a unique candle! That’s what we will show you at the beginning of the video. You will see a crazy candle idea - I bet your friends and family will be shocked the first time they see it😅 Keep watching to see a couple of DIY soap ideas you can make for your kids. We will also show how to make a Pop it soap! Stay tuned for more creative DIY ideas ;)
