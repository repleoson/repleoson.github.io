Playing on ‘checkout’ and ‘delivery’ as part of Glovo’s core business, Elizabeth Irizarry, Engineering Manager, discusses the journey that landed her as the manager of the Checkout team at Glovo in Barcelona. She focuses on the options that a career in tech provides to enable women to choose different kinds of roles (IC vs manager), different kinds of companies (start-ups vs larger companies) or even different countries (her upcoming move to BCN).
