He got the office but around11:38pm he left… he did not have very good vision, and he needed glasses to be able to drive but he left them behind on his desk. Along with his laptop and mobile phone and embarked on a 200 miles journey that didn’t make much sense…

His body was discovered shortly after 530 a.m. by a worker behind Sensenig and Weaver Well Drilling warehouse in a seriously disturbing state.
