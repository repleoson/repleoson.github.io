What is different about the Helen Doron English method in comparison with competitors?
Helen Doron describes how HDEE was the first company to teach English to babies and children using a systematic, mother-tongue methodology. Additionally, Helen Doron was the first to offer systematic teacher training courses, and thousands of educators have now been trained using the fun and very effective method. The franchise network offers original and unique materials based on over 27 years of international experience in 34 countries, which no competitor comes close to.
The international EFL (English as a Foreign Language) methodology is available for children from three months to 19 years.
