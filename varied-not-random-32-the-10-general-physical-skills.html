EPISODE NOTES:
- Adrian & Pat talk about “The 10 General Physical Skills” found in the 2002 CrossFit Journal article entitled, “What is Fitness?”.
- The 10 General Physical Skills are presented as one of several models used to define fitness.
- All 10 skills are identified & defined.
- Adrian & Pat discuss if any one skill is more important than another and/or if all 10 are of precisely the same importance.
- How the skills are developed is covered, as well as the difference between training & practice.

"What is Fitness?": https://journal.crossfit.com/article/what-is-fitness

Pat Sherwood has held credentials up to & including being a Level 4 CrossFit Coach. He worked for CrossFit, Inc for 14 years in various roles including media, health & seminar staff. He is married with 3 kids. He is the owner of CrossFit Linchpin, enjoys ice cream & hates handstand push-ups.
