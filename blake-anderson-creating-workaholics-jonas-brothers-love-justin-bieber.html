In Episode 8 of Gen2Gen Blake Anderson joins the show this week! The guys talk about how to overcome being typecast, the best concerts they’ve been to, and how Mariah Carey owning Christmas is the one thing all generations can agree on. Blake also talks about what role he would shave his hair off for, and Matthew and Erik explain how Justin Bieber helped link them up.
