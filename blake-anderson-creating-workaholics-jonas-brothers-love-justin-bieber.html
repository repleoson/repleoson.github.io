In Episode 8 of Gen2Gen Blake Anderson joins the show this week! Blake is a comedian, producer, writer, and co-creator of Workaholics, and he and Erik share some behind the scenes stories and talk about how the show came to be and why it’s so relatable. The guys talk about how to overcome being typecast, the best concerts they’ve been to, and how Mariah Carey owning Christmas is the one thing all generations can agree on. Blake also talks about what role he would shave his hair off for, and Matthew and Erik explain how Justin Bieber helped link them up.
