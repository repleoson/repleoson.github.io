Mike Proctor shares the main characteristics of the DISC Profile as it relates to helping real estate agents understand themselves and their customer, in particular, understanding how to work with people that are a high "S" - the steadiness quadrant.

High Steady Style:
Tends To Act - Patient
When In Conflict This Style - Complies
This Style Needs - Routine
Primary Drive - Stability
Preferred Tasks - Scheduled
Comfortable - Being Part of a Team
Personal Strenght - Supporter
Strength Concern - Procrastingating Addressing Change
Personal Limitation - Indecisive & Indirect
Personal Wants - Routine & Harmony
Personal Fears - Sudden Change
Blind Spot - Embracing the Need for Change
Measures Worth by - Compatability & Contributions
