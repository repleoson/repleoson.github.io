Once only a dream, the age of space tourism is upon us. This means that it might be possible for you to fly to space within your lifetime.

In the past, space tourism was only carried out by government agencies such as NASA and ESA.

But now, private companies like SpaceX, BlueOrigin, Virgin Galactic are leading this journey, thanks to entrepreneurs such as Elon Musk, Richard Branson, and Jeff Bezos

Is this an exciting proposition, or a scary one? Whatever your opinions about space travel, one thing is certain - the many advancements made in the field are also benefiting us here on Earth. This video will give you insight into where commercial space flight is at and where it is heading.
