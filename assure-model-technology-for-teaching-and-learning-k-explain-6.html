The ASSURE Model is an instructional system design that educators or teachers can use to develop lesson plans and instructions which integrate the use of technology and educational media. The ASSURE Model has six steps, A-analyze Learners, S-state objectives, S-select methods, media and materials, U-utilize methods, media, materials, R-require learner participation, E-evaluate and revise.
